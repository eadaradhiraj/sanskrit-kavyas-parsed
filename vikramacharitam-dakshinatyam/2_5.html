अदत्तदोषेण भवेद्दरिद्रो दरिद्रदोषेण करोति पापम् ।
पापादवश्यं नरकं प्रयाति पुनर्दरिद्रः पुनरेव पापी ॥ २-५॥

Translation
"One becomes poor due to the fault of not giving charity, and due to the fault of poverty, one commits sin. From sin, one inevitably goes to hell, and (upon rebirth) becomes poor again, and thus a sinner once more."
