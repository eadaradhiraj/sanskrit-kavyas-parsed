अथ संसारसंहारवामनाबन्धवासितः ।
अजायत वृषारूढो भैरवो महसां निधिः ॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
अथ अथ
Thus / Then
Grammar Indeclinable (अव्ययम्)
संसारसंहारवामनाबन्धवासितः संसार-संहार-वामन-आबन्ध-वासितः
Known/associated due to the petty obligation of the destruction of the world
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Singular. वासित: Past Passive Participle (क्त-प्रत्ययः) from root वास् (to scent/perfume/infuse).
Samasa संसारस्य संहारः (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः), संसारसंहारः एव वामनः आबन्धः (कर्मधारयः), तेन वासितः (तृतीया-तत्पुरुषः)
अजायत अजायत
Was born / Manifested
Grammar Imperfect Tense (लङ्), Atmanepada, 3rd Person, Singular - Root जन् (to be born)
वृषारूढो वृष-आरूढः
Mounted on a bull
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Singular. आरूढ: Past Passive Participle (क्त-प्रत्ययः) from root रुह् with prefix आ.
Samasa वृषम् आरूढः (द्वितीया-तत्पुरुषः)
भैरवो भैरवः
Bhairava (Lord Shiva)
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Singular
महसां महसाम्
Of lustres / Of glories
Grammar Neuter, Genitive, Plural (Base 'महस्')
निधिः निधिः
The repository / Treasure
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Singular
Translation
"Thus, the Bhairava mounted on a bull was born/manifested, the repository of lustres, known due to the petty obligation of the destruction of the world."
