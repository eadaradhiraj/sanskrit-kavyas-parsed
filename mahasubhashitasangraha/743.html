अथ कोकिल कुरु मौनं जलधरसमयेऽपि पिच्छिला भूमिः ।
विकसति कुटजकदम्बे वक्तरि भेके कुतस्तवावसरः ॥

Translation
"O Cuckoo! Keep silent during the rainy season, for even the ground is slippery (unstable). When the Kutaja and Kadamba trees are blooming and the frog has become the speaker (making loud noises), where is the scope for your sweet voice?"
(Implies: A wise person should remain silent when fools are speaking loudly or when the time is not right for their talent.)
