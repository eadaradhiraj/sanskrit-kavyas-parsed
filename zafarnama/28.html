ये सन्ति सज्जया हीनाः श्येनैः सैन्यैः भुवा नराः ।
तेभ्योऽपि भगवान् दाता किल ऐश्वर्यापवर्गयोः ॥ २८ ॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
ये ये
Those who
Grammar Pronoun (Base 'यद्'), Masculine, Nominative, Plural
सन्ति सन्ति
Are
Grammar Root 'अस्' (to be), Lat Lakara (Present Tense), 3rd Person, Plural
सज्जया सज्जया
By equipment / accoutrements / saaj-saamaan
Grammar Feminine, Instrumental, Singular (Base 'सज्जा')
हीनाः हीनाः
Devoid of / without
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Plural (Base 'हीन', root 'हा')
श्येनैः श्येनैः
By hawks / falcons
Grammar Masculine, Instrumental, Plural (Base 'श्येन')
सैन्यैः सैन्यैः
By armies
Grammar Instrumental, Plural (Base 'सैन्य')
(Note: Printed as 'सैन्यै' without visarga due to typographical omission)
भुवा भुवा
By land / territory
Grammar Feminine, Instrumental, Singular (Base 'भू')
नराः नराः
Men / people
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Plural (Base 'नर')

Even to them
Grammar तेभ्यः: Pronoun (Base 'तद्'), Masculine, Dative, Plural
अपि: Indeclinable (Emphasis)
भगवान् भगवान्
The Lord / God
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Singular (Base 'भगवत्')
दाता दाता
The giver / bestower
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Singular (Base 'दातृ')
किलैश्वर्यापवर्गयोः किल ऐश्वर्य-अपवर्गयोः
Indeed, of sovereignty and liberation (heavenly bliss)
Grammar किल: Indeclinable
ऐश्वर्य-अपवर्गयोः: Dual, Dative/Genitive
Samasa ऐश्वर्यं च अपवर्गः च इति ऐश्वर्यापवर्गौ (इतरेतर-द्वन्द्वः), तयोः।
Translation
Hindi: वे लोग जिनके पास न कोई साज़-सामान (सज्जा) है, न कोई बाज (शिकारी पक्षी) है, न कोई सेना है और न ही अपनी कोई धरती है; भगवान् उन पर भी कृपा करके उन्हें परम ऐश्वर्य और स्वर्ग (मुक्ति) प्रदान करने वाला है।

English: Those who are without any accoutrements, without hawks, without armies, and without any land of their own—God indeed lavishes upon even them his boons of sovereignty (worldly wealth) and heavenly bliss (liberation).
