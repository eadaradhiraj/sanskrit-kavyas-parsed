किमस्ति यदि जम्बूकश्छलेन कपटेन वा ।
एवं बत समासाद्य हतवान् सिंहशावकौ ॥ १४ ॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
किमस्ति किम् अस्ति
What is there / What matters
Grammar किम्: Interrogative Pronoun, Neuter, Nominative, Singular
अस्ति: Root 'अस्' (to be), Lat Lakara (Present), 3rd Person, Singular
यदि यदि
If
Grammar Indeclinable (अव्ययम्)
जम्बूकश्छलेन जम्बूकः छलेन
The jackal, through deceit
Grammar जम्बूकः: Masculine, Nominative, Singular (Jackal)
छलेन: Neuter, Instrumental, Singular (Deceit)
कपटेन कपटेन
Through treachery / fraud
Grammar Masculine, Instrumental, Singular (Base 'कपट')
वा वा
Or
Grammar Indeclinable (अव्ययम्)
एवं एवम्
Thus / In this way
Grammar Indeclinable (अव्ययम्)
बत बत
Alas / Indeed (particle of regret/sarcasm)
Grammar Indeclinable (अव्ययम्)
समासाद्य समासाद्य
Having approached / encountered
Grammar Prefixes 'सम्' and 'आ' + Root 'सद्', Lyap-Pratyaya (Gerund)
हतवान् हतवान्
Killed
Grammar Root 'हन्' (to kill), Ktavatu-Pratyaya (Active Past Participle), Masculine, Nominative, Singular
सिंहशावकौ सिंह-शावकौ
Two lion cubs
Grammar Masculine, Accusative, Dual
Samasa सिंहस्य शावकौ इति सिंह-शावकौ (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः), तौ ।
Translation
Hindi: क्या हुआ यदि सियार ने छल और कपट से इस तरह शेर के दो बच्चों (साहिबजादों) को मार दिया।

English: What matters if a jackal through deceit and treachery thus killed two cubs of a lion.
