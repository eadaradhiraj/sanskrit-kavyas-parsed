कललेन त्वया तेन चाममाधारमाधृतम् ।
आत्मनो भवनं कर्तुं मैश्वर्यस्य सुखस्य च ॥ ८ ॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
कललेन कललेन
With mortar / mire (of blood and earth)
Grammar Masculine, Instrumental, Singular
त्वया त्वया
By you
Grammar Pronoun (Base 'युष्मद्'), Instrumental, Singular
तेन तेन
By that
Grammar Masculine, Instrumental, Singular (Base 'तद्')
चाममाधारमाधृतम् च आमम् आधारम् आधृतम्
And a flimsy/raw base was supported
Grammar आमम्: Raw / Unbaked / Flimsy (Adj to आधारम्)
आधारम्: Base / Foundation (Accusative Sg.)
आधृतम्: Supported / Laid (Kta-pratyaya)
आत्मनो आत्मनः
Of your own
Grammar Genitive, Singular (Base 'आत्मन्')
भवनं भवनम्
Abode / Palace / Building
Grammar Neuter, Accusative, Singular
कर्तुम्
To make / create
Grammar Tumun-pratyaya (Infinitive) from root 'कृ'
ऐश्वर्यस्य
Of (your) opulence / power
Grammar Sandhi: च + आमम् (from line 1) and here 'मैश्वर्यस्य' implies the intent of luxury.
ऐश्वर्यस्य: Neuter, Genitive, Singular
सुखस्य सुखस्य
Of pleasure
Grammar Neuter, Genitive, Singular
च च
And
Grammar Indeclinable
Translation
Hindi: और उससे (रक्त और मिट्टी के गारे से) तूने कच्चे घर की नींव रखी - अपना ऐश्वर्य भवन बनाने के लिये।

English: With such (sanguine) mortar thou laid’st a flimsy base of an abode of pleasure of thy own.
