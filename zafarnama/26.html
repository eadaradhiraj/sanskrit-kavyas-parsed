जगतः शरणं दाता तथा हस्तस्य ग्राहकः ।
क्षन्ता सर्वापराधस्य जीविकादो मनोहरः ॥ २६ ॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
जगतः जगतः
Of the universe / world
Grammar Neuter, Genitive, Singular (Base 'जगत्')
शरणं शरणम्
Refuge / shelter
Grammar Neuter, Nominative/Accusative, Singular (Base 'शरण')
दाता दाता
Giver / benefactor
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Singular (Base 'दातृ', root 'दा')

Holder of the hand (Helper/Saviour)
Grammar हस्तस्य: Masculine, Genitive, Singular (of the hand)
ग्राहकः: Masculine, Nominative, Singular (one who holds / takes)
क्षन्ता क्षन्ता
Forgiver / pardoner / condoner
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Singular (Base 'क्षन्तृ', root 'क्षम्')

Grammar Masculine, Genitive, Singular

जीविकादो जीविका-दः
Bestower of livelihood / bread
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Singular
Samasa जीविकां ददाति इति जीविका-दः (उपपद-तत्पुरुषः)
मनोहरः मनः-हरः
Charming / pleasing (one who wins the heart)
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Singular
Samasa मनः हरति इति मनः-हरः (उपपद-तत्पुरुषः)
Translation
Hindi: वह (परमात्मा) समस्त संसार को शरण देने वाला है, महादाता है और सबका सहायक (हाथ पकड़ने वाला) है। वह समस्त अपराधों को क्षमा करने वाला, सब जीवों को जीविका प्रदान करने वाला और मन को आनंदित करने वाला है।

English: He grants refuge to the universe, is the Giver (benefactor), and holds the hand of the helpless (helpful). He is the forgiver of all sins, the provider of livelihood, and the delighter of hearts.
