यतश्चासन-शोभेभ्यो नाप्नुवन्ति जनाश्छलम् ॥ ५ ॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
न न
Not
Grammar Indeclinable (Negative Particle)
शोभते शोभते
Suits / Beseems / Looks good
Grammar Root 'शुभ्', Atmanepada, Lat Lakara (Present Tense), 3rd Person, Singular
पुनस्तुभ्यं पुनः तुभ्यम्
However, to you
Grammar पुनः: Indeclinable
तुभ्यम्: Pronoun (Base 'युष्मद्'), Dative, Singular
नाम नाम
The name
Grammar Neuter, Nominative, Singular
चासन-शोभनः च आसन-शोभनः
And 'Aurangzeb' (Adornment of the throne)
Grammar च: Indeclinable
आसन-शोभनः: Masculine, Nominative, Singular
Samasa आसनस्य शोभनः (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः) - Note: Sanskritization of "Aurang-zeb".
यतश्चासन-शोभेभ्यो यतः च आसन-शोभेभ्यः
Because and from 'Aurangzebs' (Kings)
Grammar यतः: Indeclinable (Because)
आसन-शोभेभ्यः: Masculine, Ablative, Plural
Samasa आसनस्य शोभः (Adornment of throne/King); तेभ्यः (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः)
नाप्नुवन्ति न आप्नुवन्ति
Do not obtain / find
Grammar आप्नुवन्ति: Root 'आप्', Parasmaipada, Lat Lakara (Present Tense), 3rd Person, Plural
जनाश्छलम् जनाः छलम्
People, deceit
Grammar जनाः: Masculine, Nominative, Plural
छलम्: Neuter, Accusative, Singular
Translation
Hindi: तुम्हें औरंगज़ेब (आसन-शोभा) नाम शोभा नहीं देता, (क्योंकि) औरंगज़ेबों से (सच्चे राजाओं से) लोग छल (धोखा) प्राप्त नहीं करते हैं।

English: Ill becometh thee thy name, O Aurangzeb! (Adornment of the Throne); for deceit is not to be found from those who are true adornments of the throne (kings).

Note: Aurang = Asana (Throne), Zeb = Shobhana (Adornment).
