Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
त्वमसि त्वम् असि
You are

असि: Root 'अस्' (to be), Present Tense (लट्), 2nd Person, Singular
(Note: The printed text has a typo 'त्वमस्ति' with 3rd person 'अस्ति' instead of 'असि').

अहम्: Pronoun, Nominative, Singular (अस्मद्)
सिंहमुक्तं सिंहम् मुक्तम्
The lion, set free
Grammar सिंहम्: Masculine, Accusative, Singular (Base 'सिंह')
मुक्तम्: Masculine, Accusative, Singular (Past Participle from root 'मुच्')
पञ्जरम् पञ्जरम्
From the cage / the cage
Grammar Neuter, Accusative/Nominative Singular (Base 'पञ्जर')
Translation
Hindi: तू यदि वयोवृद्ध अनुभवी भेड़िया है, तो मैं भी सिंह को (सिंह नामधारी शिष्य-सिख सम्प्रदाय को) पिंजरे (जाल) से बाहर खुला-मुक्त रखता हूँ।

English: If thou art weather-wise or an age-old wolf, I too have my lion untied and set free of the cage.
