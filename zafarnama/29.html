पुनीता पृथिवी यस्याधस्ताद् ध्रुवं परिस्थिता ।
वसून् ददाति सः साक्षादुपस्थित इवानिशम् ॥ २९ ॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
पुनीता पुनीता
Pure / holy / sacred
Grammar Feminine, Nominative, Singular (Adjective qualifying पृथिवी)
पृथिवी पृथिवी
The earth
Grammar Feminine, Nominative, Singular (Base 'पृथिवी')
यस्याधस्ताद् यस्य अधस्तात्
Underneath whom / under whose authority
Grammar यस्य: Pronoun (Base 'यद्'), Masculine, Genitive, Singular
अधस्तात्: Indeclinable (underneath / below)
ध्रुवं ध्रुवम्
Firmly / eternally / constantly
Grammar Indeclinable (used adverbially)
परिस्थिता परिस्थिता
Established / situated
Grammar Past Passive Participle (Kta) of root 'स्था' with prefix 'परि', Feminine, Nominative, Singular
वसून् वसून्
Treasures / wealth / riches
Grammar Masculine, Accusative, Plural (Base 'वसु')
ददाति ददाति
Gives / bestows
Grammar Root 'दा', Juhotyadi-gaṇa, Lat Lakara (Present Tense), 3rd Person, Singular
सः सः
He
Grammar Pronoun (Base 'तद्'), Masculine, Nominative, Singular
साक्षादुपस्थित साक्षात् उपस्थितः
Manifested in person, present
Grammar साक्षात्: Indeclinable (In person / manifestly)
उपस्थितः: Past Passive Participle of root 'स्था' with prefix 'उप', Masculine, Nominative, Singular
इवानिशम् इव अनिशम्
As if, ceaselessly (day and night)
Grammar इव: Indeclinable (Comparison particle)
अनिशम्: Indeclinable (Adverbial - ceaselessly)
Translation
Hindi: जिसके (परमात्मा के) नीचे यह पवित्र पृथ्वी दृढ़तापूर्वक स्थित है; वह सदा साक्षात् सम्मुख उपस्थित के समान निरंतर सबको धन-सम्पत्ति (वसून्) प्रदान करता रहता है।

English: Underneath whose authority this Holy Earth is firmly established, and from whom He ceaselessly endows treasures to all, as if present in person.
