यावत् सिंहो महाशूरो जीवितश्चेह विद्यते ।
प्रतिशोधमतस्तत्तो ह्यवश्यं स करिष्यति ॥ १५ ॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
यावत् यावत्
As long as
Grammar Indeclinable (अव्ययम्)
सिंहो सिंहः
The lion (referring to Guru Gobind Singh)
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Singular
महाशूरो महा-शूरः
The mighty brave / valiant
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Singular (Adjective to सिंहः)
Samasa महान् शूरः इति महाशूरः (विशेषण-पूर्वपद-कर्मधारयः)
जीवितश्चेह जीवितः च इह
Alive and here (in this world)
Grammar जीवितः: Nominative Sg. (Living)
च, इह: Indeclinables
विद्यते विद्यते
Exists / remains
Grammar Root 'विद्' (vid) to exist, Passive/Atmanepada, Lat Lakara (Present Tense), 3rd Person, Singular
प्रतिशोधमतस्तत्तो प्रतिशोधम् अतः त्वत्तः
Revenge therefore from you
Grammar प्रतिशोधम्: Accusative Sg. (Revenge)
अतः: Indeclinable (Therefore)
त्वत्तः: Pronoun (युष्मद्), Ablative Singular (from you - suffix -tasi).
(Note: The printed text has a typo 'तत्तो' for 'त्वत्तः' / 'त्वत्तो' meaning "from you").
ह्यवश्यं हि अवश्यम्
Indeed certainly
Grammar हि: Indeclinable (Indeed)
अवश्यम्: Indeclinable (Certainly)
स सः
He
Grammar Pronoun (Base 'तद्'), Masculine, Nominative, Singular
करिष्यति करिष्यति
Will inflict / take
Grammar Root 'कृ' (to do/perform), Lrt Lakara (Future Tense), 3rd Person, Singular
Translation
Hindi: जब तक वह वीर शेर (गुरु गोविंद सिंह) जीवित रहता है, तब तक वह तुमसे अवश्य ही प्रतिशोध लेगा।

English: As long as the brave lion is alive (on hoof), he will indeed certainly inflict retribution (take revenge) on thee.
