अत्यपूर्वस्य रागस्य पूर्वपक्षाय पल्लवाः ।
पद्मानि पादयुग्मस्य प्रत्युदाहरणानि च ॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis

Samasa पादयोः युग्मं, तस्य (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः)
प्रत्युदाहरणानि प्रति-उदाहरणानि
Counter-examples / Contrary examples
Samasa प्रतिकूलानि उदाहरणानि (प्रादि-समासः / कर्मधारयः)
च च
And
Translation
"The new leaves (pallavas) serve merely as the 'prima facie argument' (Purvapaksha - an inferior view to be refuted) for the extremely unprecedented redness of her lips. And the lotuses serve as 'counter-examples' (Pratyudaharana - negative examples) for her pair of feet."
(Meaning: Her lips are far redder than new leaves, and her feet are far more beautiful than lotuses, making the comparisons with leaves and lotuses seem like weak arguments in logic.)
