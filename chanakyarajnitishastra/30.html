अतोऽर्थं पठ्यते शास्त्रं कीर्तिर्लोकेषु जायते ।
कीर्तिमान् पूज्यते लोके परत्रेह च मानवः ॥

Translation
"Therefore, the scripture of economics (Artha) is studied, whereby fame is generated in the worlds. A famous man is honoured both in this world and in the next."
