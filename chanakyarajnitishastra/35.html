अदीप्तेऽग्नौ हतो होमो हता भुक्तिरसाक्षिका ।
उपजीव्या हता कन्या स्वार्थे पाकक्रिया हता ॥ ३५॥

The action of cooking
Grammar Feminine, Nominative, Singular
Samasa पाकस्य क्रिया (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः)
हता हता
Ruined / Void of merit
Grammar Feminine, Nominative, Singular. Past Passive Participle (क्त-प्रत्ययः) from root हन्.
Translation
"The fire-sacrifice performed in an unignited fire (without a flame) is ruined (fruitless). The enjoyment of property devoid of a witness is ruined (rendered legally void). A daughter who is made a means of livelihood by her family is ruined (the virtue of Kanyadana is destroyed). And the act of cooking done purely out of selfishness is ruined (devoid of any merit)."
