प्रातश्श्रुतमाह्वानम् शुण्डायास्तिष्ठ रे सुरापायिन् ।
पूरय पात्रं तूर्णं न यावदसुभाजनं भवति पूर्णम् ॥

Translation
"In the morning, the call of the tavern was heard: 'Stop, O drinker of wine! Fill the cup quickly, before the vessel of life becomes full (and reaches its end).'"
