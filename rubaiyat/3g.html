मधुवारोऽयम् प्रातः पायय मां पात्रि कुक्कुटोरौति ।
पानस्य नौ सुराया अहो बतात्रैकदा समावेशः ॥
नहि दीक्षावसरोऽयम् मा शोचीरीदृशं वृथा पात्रि ।
धर्मस्य काद्य चिन्ता पिब हालासारमखिलमपि पात्रि ॥

Translation
"This is the occasion for wine in the morning; make me drink, O cup-bearer, for the cock cries! Oh, what a coming together here of drinking, of us two, and of the wine! This is indeed not the time for religious vows; do not grieve like this in vain, O cup-bearer! What is the worry about dharma today? Drink the entire essence of the wine, O cup-bearer!"
