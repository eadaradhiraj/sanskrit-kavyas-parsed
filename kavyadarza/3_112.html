अत्रोद्याने मया दृष्टा वल्लरी पञ्चपल्लवा ।
पल्लवे पल्लवे ताम्रा यस्यां कुसुममञ्जरी ॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
अत्र अत्र
Here
उद्याने उद्याने
In the garden (Metaphor: Body)
मया मया
By me
दृष्टा दृष्टा
Was seen
वल्लरी वल्लरी
A creeper (Metaphor: Hand/Arm)
पञ्चपल्लवा पञ्च-पल्लवा
Having five sprouts (Metaphor: Five fingers)
Samasa पञ्च पल्लवाः यस्याः सा (बहुव्रीहिः)
पल्लवे पल्लवे पल्लवे पल्लवे
On every sprout (finger)
Grammar Vipsa (Repetition implies 'each')
ताम्रा ताम्रा
Copper-colored / Red
यस्यां यस्यां
In/On which
कुसुममञ्जरी कुसुम-मञ्जरी
Cluster of flowers (Metaphor: Nails)
Samasa कुसुमानां मञ्जरी (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः)
Translation
"In the garden here (the heroine's body), I saw a creeper (hand) with five sprouts (fingers); on each sprout of which there was a copper-colored cluster of flowers (red nails)."
