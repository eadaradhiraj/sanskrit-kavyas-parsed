अन्योक्तिसूक्तमुक्तालीं समुद्धृत्य श्रुताम्बुधेः ॥ १-२५॥

From the ocean of heard knowledge / scriptures
Grammar Masculine, Ablative, Singular (Base 'अम्बुधि')
Samasa श्रुतम् एव अम्बुधिः (कर्मधारयः / रूपकः), तस्मात्
Translation
"Now, here in the literature, having extracted rows of pearls of allegorical quotes from the ocean of heard knowledge (discourses), sequential gates (sections) are being constructed."
