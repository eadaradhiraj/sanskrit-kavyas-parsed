अनभ्यासहतोत्साहा परेण परिभूयते ।
या लज्जाजननी जाड्यात्किं तया मन्दविद्यया ॥ ३-४०
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
अनभ्यासहतोत्साहा अनभ्यास-हत-उत्साहा
Knowledge whose power is destroyed due to lack of practice
Grammar Feminine, Nominative, Singular (Adjective qualifying 'विद्या')
Samasa न अभ्यासः इति अनभ्यासः (नञ्-तत्पुरुषः) । हतः उत्साहः यस्याः सा हतोत्साहा (बहुव्रीहिः) । अनभ्यासेन हतोत्साहा इति अनभ्यास-हतोत्साहा (तृतीया-तत्पुरुषः) ।
परेण परेण
By another / by the enemy
Grammar Masculine, Instrumental, Singular (Base 'पर')
परिभूयते परिभूयते
Is scorned / defeated / overcome
Grammar Root 'भू' with prefix 'परि' (to insult/overcome), Passive Voice (कर्मणि), Lat Lakara (Present Tense), 3rd Person, Singular
या या
Which (knowledge)
Grammar Feminine, Nominative, Singular (Pronoun 'यद्')
लज्जाजननी लज्जा-जननी
The mother (producer) of embarrassment / shame
Grammar Feminine, Nominative, Singular
Samasa लज्जायाः जननी इति लज्जा-जननी (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः)
जाड्यात्किं जाड्यात् किम्
Due to dullness / apathy, what (use is there)
Grammar जाड्यात्: Neuter, Ablative, Singular (Base 'जाड्य')
किम्: Interrogative pronoun, Neuter, Nominative, Singular
तया तया
By that / with that
Grammar Feminine, Instrumental, Singular (Pronoun 'तद्')
मन्दविद्यया मन्द-विद्यया
With feeble / meager knowledge
Grammar Feminine, Instrumental, Singular
Samasa मन्दा चासौ विद्या च इति मन्द-विद्या (कर्मधारयः), तया ।
Translation

English: Knowledge whose power is destroyed due to lack of practice is easily scorned (or defeated) by others. What indeed is the use of such meager knowledge which, due to dullness or apathy, only acts as the mother of embarrassment?

Hindi: अभ्यास न करने के कारण जिस विद्या का उत्साह (शक्ति) नष्ट हो चुका है, वह दूसरों के द्वारा आसानी से तिरस्कृत की जाती है। ऐसी मंद (अल्प) विद्या से क्या लाभ, जो जड़ता या प्रमाद के कारण केवल लज्जा उत्पन्न करने वाली (लज्जा की जननी) होती है?
