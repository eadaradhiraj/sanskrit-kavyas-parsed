तां रोहिणीं विजानीहि ज्योतिषामत्र मण्डले ।
यस्तन्वि तारकान्यासः शकटाकारमाश्रितः ॥

अत्र मन्मथमिवातिसुन्दरं दानवारिमिव दिव्यतेजसम् ।
शैलराजमिव धैर्यशालिनं वेद्मि वेङ्कटपतिं महीपतिम् ॥

King Venkatapati
Translation & Commentary

Example 1: "O slender one! Know that to be Rohini in this circle of stars—that particular arrangement of stars which has assumed the shape of a cart."

Example 2: "Here, I recognize King Venkatapati—who is extremely beautiful like Manmatha (Cupid), possessed of divine luster like the Enemy of Demons (Vishnu), and endowed with fortitude like the King of Mountains (Himalaya)."
