Budhbhushan

Shloka 5
या भवेक्षणचकोरचन्द्रिका शांभवी निजजनस्य पालिका ।
या तुषारगिरिराजकन्यका सा ममास्तु शुभकालमालिका ॥
Padaccheda & Padartha (Analysis)
या या
She who
भवेक्षणचकोरचन्द्रिका भव-ईक्षण-चकोर-चन्द्रिका
Moonlight for the chakora birds which are the eyes of the world
Samasa Vigraha भवस्य ईक्षणानि एव चकोराः तेषां चन्द्रिका
शांभवी शांभवी
Shambhavi (Wife of Shambhu)
निजजनस्य निज-जनस्य
Of her own people
Samasa Vigraha निजानां जनानाम्
पालिका पालिका
The protector
या या
She who
तुषारगिरिराजकन्यका तुषार-गिरि-राज-कन्यका
Daughter of the King of Snowy Mountains (Himalaya)
Samasa Vigraha तुषारगिरेः राज्ञः कन्यका
सा सा
She
मम मम
My
अस्तु अस्तु
Let be
शुभकालमालिका शुभ-काल-मालिका
The provider of auspicious times
Samasa Vigraha शुभस्य कालस्य मालिका
"May She, Shambhavi, who is like moonlight to the chakora birds that are the eyes of the world, who is the protector of her own people, and who is the daughter of the King of the Himalayas, be the provider of a succession of auspicious times for me."
