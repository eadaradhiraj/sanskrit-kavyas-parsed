विरम तिमिर साहसाद् अमुष्माद्
रविरस्तमितो गतस्ततः किम् ।
कलयसि न पुरो महोत्सवोर्मि-
र्द्युतिनिधिरभ्युदितोऽयं शशाङ्कः ॥ ६९ ॥

Translation
"Desist, O Darkness, from this rashness! (You think) 'The Sun has set', so what? Do you not see in front of you that this Moon has risen—a repository of light and a wave of great festivity?"
