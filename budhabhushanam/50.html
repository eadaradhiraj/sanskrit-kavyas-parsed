शङ्करः शङ्करः
Lord Shankara
Translation
"May that Śaṅkara protect you—he who is an expert in the miraculous politics (of balancing contradictions). For he digested the mouthful of potent Kālakūṭa poison; he forcefully burnt Kāmadeva; he reduced the cosmic fire of the apocalypse (Kalpānta) to the small flame in the eye on his shining forehead; and yet, adorned with power, he simultaneously holds the Moon (coolness), the Daughter of the Mountain (Pārvatī), and the Celestial River (Gaṅgā)."
