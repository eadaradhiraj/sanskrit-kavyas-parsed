औत्सुक्यात्तरलाः स्मराद्विकसिता भीत्या समाकुञ्चिताः
क्रोधेन ज्वलिता मुदा मुकुलिताः शौरेर्दृशः पान्तु वः ॥५५॥

Translation
"May the glances of Shauri (Vishnu) protect you—glances that were restless with curiosity, blossomed with love, narrowed with fear, inflamed with anger, and half-closed with joy as He gazed upon the lotus-face of Lakshmī, which was highly agitated by the ocean waves swirling within the deep caves of the revolving Mandara mountain."
