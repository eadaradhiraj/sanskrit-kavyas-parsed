करान्प्रसार्य रविणा दक्षिणाशावलम्बिना ।
न केवलं लम्बमानेनात्मा दिवसोऽपि लघूकृतः ॥ ६५ ॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
करान् करान्
Rays / Hands
प्रसार्य प्रसार्य
Having extended / Stretched out
Grammar Absolutive (LyaP)
रविणा रविणा
By the Sun
दक्षिणाशावलम्बिना दक्षिण-आशा-अवलम्बिना
Depending on the Southern direction (or: relying on charity/Dakshina)
Samasa दक्षिणां आशां (दिशं) अवलम्बते इति (उपपद-तत्पुरुषः)
न केवलम् न केवलम्
Not only
लम्बमानेन लम्बमानेन
By him hanging down / sinking low
आत्मा आत्मा
Himself
दिवसः दिवसः
The day
अपि अपि
Also
लघूकृतः लघू-कृतः
Made short / Belittled
Translation
"By extending his rays (hands) while leaning towards the South (Dakshinayana), the sinking Sun has not only belittled himself (by going low), but has also made the day short."
(Metaphor: A man who extends his hands for charity/Dakshina and depends on others belittles himself and shortens his glory).
