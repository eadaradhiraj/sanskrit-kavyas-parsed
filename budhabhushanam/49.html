Translation
"O Vāṇi (Saraswati), may that gentle smile of yours be for my prosperity—the smile by which the parrot was stopped as it continuously pecked at the crystal rosary in your lotus-hand, mistaking the beads for pomegranate seeds because they were reddened by the radiance of your nails."
