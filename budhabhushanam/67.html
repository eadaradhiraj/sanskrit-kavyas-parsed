पातः पूष्णो भवति महते नोपतापाय यस्मात्
कालेनास्तं क इह न गता यान्ति यास्यन्ति चान्ये ।
एतावत्तु व्यथयति यदालोकबाह्यैस्तमोभि-
स्तस्मिन्नेव प्रकृतिमहति व्योम्नि लब्धोऽवकाशः ॥ ६७ ॥

Translation
"The fall of the Sun is great (or happens even to the great); it is not a cause for distress, because who here has not met their end in due time? Others go and will go. But this much causes pain: that darkness, which is alien to light, has found space in that very sky which is by nature great."
