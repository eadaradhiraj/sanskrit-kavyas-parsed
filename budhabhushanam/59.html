Of Lakshmi
पुनातु पुनातु
May it purify
Translation
"May the body of Lakshmi purify you—the body which, as she was rising after love-making, placing her weight (`भरम्`) with one hand on the Lord of Serpents and holding her garment with the other, carrying her loosened hair-knot on her shoulder, was embraced and pulled back to the bed by Shauri (Vishnu), whose passion was doubled by her beauty at that moment."
