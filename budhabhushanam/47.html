Translation

(Shiva): "O Dearest, why is there such sweat on you?"
(Parvati): "Because of the heat from the fire of your (third) eye!"

(Shiva): "O Moon-faced one, from what cause is this trembling?"
(Parvati): "O Lord (Bhava), from the fear of the King of Snakes (Vasuki, around your neck)!"

(Shiva): "O Devi, why do you have goosebumps?"
(Parvati): "O Worshipful One, due to the cold spray of water from the clouds (Nagas)!"

In this way, may Gauri, who is intent on concealing her true feelings (of love/excitement, known as Sattvika Bhavas) from her husband using clever puns, protect us for a long time.
