विद्राणे रुद्रवृन्दे सवितरि तरले वज्रिणि ध्वस्तवज्रे
जाताशङ्के शशाङ्के विरमति मरुति त्यक्तवैरे कुबेरे ।
वैकुण्ठे कुण्ठितास्त्रे महिषमतिरुषा पौरुषोपघ्ननिघ्नं
निर्विघ्नं निघ्नती वः शमयतु दुरितं भूरिभावा भवानी ॥४८॥

Translation
"When the host of Rudras had fled, when the Sun was trembling, when Indra's thunderbolt was destroyed, when the Moon was struck with fear, when the Wind had ceased blowing, when Kubera had abandoned his hostility, and when Vishnu's weapons were blunted—may that Goddess Bhavani, full of profound affection, who killed Mahishasura (who was relying solely on his brute strength) effortlessly and with great anger, pacify your calamities."
