कृष्ण त्वं नवयौवनोऽसि चपला प्रायेण गोपाङ्गना
कंसो भूपतिरब्जनालमृदुलग्रीवा वयं गोदुहः ।
तद्याचेऽञ्जलिना भवन्तमधुना वृन्दावनं माऽधुना
मा यासीरिति नन्दगोपवचसा नम्रो हरिः पातु वः ॥५४॥

Translation
"O Kṛṣṇa, you are fresh with youth; the cowherd women (Gopikās) are generally fickle; King Kaṃsa is cruel, and we are simple milkmaids with necks as tender as lotus stalks. Therefore, I beg you with folded hands—do not go to Vṛndāvana now." May Lord Hari, who stood humbled/bent by these affectionate words of Nanda Gopa, protect you all.
