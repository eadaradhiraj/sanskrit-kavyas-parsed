कलिकालभुजङ्गमावलीढं निखिलं धर्ममवेक्ष्य विक्लवं यः ।
जगतः पतिरंशतोवतीर्णः स शिवच्छत्रपतिर्जयत्यजेयः ॥९॥
Padaccheda & Padartha (Analysis)
कलिकालभुजङ्गमावलीढं कलिकाल-भुजङ्गम-आवलीढं
Bitten (swallowed) by the serpent in the form of the Kali age
Samasa कलिकाल-रूपिणा भुजङ्गमेन आवलीढम्
निखिलं धर्ममवेक्ष्य निखिलं धर्मम् अवेक्ष्य
Having seen the entire Dharma (Religion)
विक्लवं यः विक्लवं यः
Which was in distress/panic; Who (referring to Shivaji)
जगतः पतिः जगतः पतिः
The Lord of the World (Vishnu)
अंशतोवतीर्णः अंशतस्-अवतीर्णः
Incarnated partially (Assumed a partial incarnation)
स शिवच्छत्रपतिः सः शिव-छत्रपतिः
That Chhatrapati Shivaji
जयत्यजेयः जयति अजेयः
Victorious is the invincible one
"Victorious as Śiva, the invincible Chatrapati, Lord of the Umbrella, who is none but the Lord of the World (God Viṣṇu) who has assumed a partial incarnation, having seen the whole religion in distress as it was bitten by the venomous serpent in the form of the Kali age."
