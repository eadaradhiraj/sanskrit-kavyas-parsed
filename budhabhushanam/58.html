Translation
"May that Lord Hari in his Boar Incarnation (Varaha)—who diminishes the joy in the minds of the wives of the arrogant demons—make the world safe and imperishable. The Earth, resting very securely in the interior of the curved hollow of the tip of his tusk, shines like a female bee clinging to a white Ketaka petal."
