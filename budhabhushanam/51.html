Samasa कात्यायन्याः कामुकः (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः)
Translation
"May that Lover of Kātyāyanī (Shiva) protect you—He who has transformed the Lord of Serpents (Vasuki) into his armlet, bracelet, hair-ornament, bowstring, earring, and waistband; and who has transformed the crescent moon into his forehead mark (Tilaka), a mirror for his beloved, his eye, and the stake wagered at the start of a dice game."
