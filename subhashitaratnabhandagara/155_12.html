वनानि दहतो वह्नेः सखा भवति मारुतः ।
स एव दीपनाशाय कृशे कस्यास्ति सौहृदम् ॥

सौहृदम् सौहृदम्

Grammar Neuter, Nominative, Singular (Abstract noun derived from 'सुहृद्')
Translation
"The wind becomes a friend to the fire that is burning down entire forests, but that very same wind serves to extinguish a small glowing lamp. Indeed, who holds friendship with the weak?"
