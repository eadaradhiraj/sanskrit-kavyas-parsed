तदिहानवधौ निमज्जतो मम कन्दर्पशराधिनीरधौ ।
भव पोत इवावलम्बनं विधिनाकस्मिकसृष्टसंनिधिः ॥ २-६०॥

Of the one drowning / sinking
Grammar Genitive, Singular. Present Active Participle (शतृ-प्रत्ययः) from root नि + मज्ज् (to drown).

Grammar Neuter, Nominative/Accusative, Singular
विधिना विधिना
By fate
Grammar Masculine, Instrumental, Singular (Base 'विधि')
आकस्मिकसृष्टसंनिधिः आकस्मिक-सृष्ट-संनिधिः
(You) whose presence was unexpectedly created
Grammar सृष्ट: Past Passive Participle (क्त-प्रत्ययः) from root सृज् (to create).
Samasa आकस्मिकी सृष्टा संनिधिः यस्य सः (बहुव्रीहिः)
Translation
"Therefore, for me who am sinking here in the boundless ocean which is the quiver of Cupid (Kamadeva), may you, whose presence has been unexpectedly brought about by fate, become a support like a boat."
