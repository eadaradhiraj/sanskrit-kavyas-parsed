अनया तव रूपसीमया कृतसंस्कारविबोधनस्य मे ।
चिरमप्यवलोकिताद्य सा स्मृतिमारूढवती शुचिस्मिता ॥ २-४३
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
अनया अनया
By this
Grammar Feminine, Instrumental, Singular (Pronoun 'इदम्')
तव तव
Your
Grammar Pronoun, Genitive, Singular (Base 'युष्मद्')

By the pinnacle / extreme boundary of beauty
Grammar Feminine, Instrumental, Singular
Samasa रूपस्य सीमा इति रूपसीमा (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः), तया ।
कृतसंस्कारविबोधनस्य कृत-संस्कार-विबोधनस्य
Of the one whose latent impressions (recollections) have been awakened
Grammar Masculine, Genitive, Singular (Adjective qualifying 'मे')

Even though seen a very long time ago, today

स्मृतिमारूढवती स्मृतिम् आरूढवती
Has entered / ascended into memory
Grammar स्मृतिम्: Feminine, Accusative, Singular (Base 'स्मृति')
आरूढवती: Feminine, Nominative, Singular (Active Past Participle / क्तवतु of root 'रुह्' with prefix 'आ')
शुचिस्मिता शुचि-स्मिता
That holy (pure) smile-bearing one
Grammar Feminine, Nominative, Singular
Samasa शुचिः स्मिता (मन्दहास्यः) यस्याः सा (बहुव्रीहिः)
Translation

English: Upon seeing this extreme pinnacle of your beauty, that pure, holy-smiling one (Damayanti) who was seen by me a very long time ago, has today ascended into the memory of mine—whose latent impressions have been thus awakened.

Hindi: तुम्हारे रूप की इस अनुपम पराकाष्ठा (सौन्दर्य-सीमा) को देखकर, मेरे मन में सुप्त पड़े पुराने संस्कार फिर से जाग उठे हैं; और बहुत पहले देखी गई वह पवित्र एवं मधुर मुस्कान वाली सुन्दरी (दमयन्ती) आज अचानक मेरी स्मृति में साकार हो उठी है।
