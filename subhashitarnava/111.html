अत्युन्नतोऽम्बुभिर्मेघश्चातकान्न धिनोति चेत् ।
मरुता हृतसर्वस्वः स पश्चात्किं करिष्यति ॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
अत्युन्नतः अति-उन्नतः

सः सः
He (That cloud)
पश्चात् पश्चात्

What?
करिष्यति करिष्यति
Will he do?
Translation
"If a very lofty cloud does not quench the Chataka birds (Jacobin cuckoos) with its waters, then later, having lost everything (its water being scattered) because of the wind — what will it be able to do?"
(Implies: A wealthy person should help the needy while they have the capacity, for once fortune is lost, regret is useless.)
