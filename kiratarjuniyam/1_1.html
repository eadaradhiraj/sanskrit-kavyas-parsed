श्रियः कुरूणामधिपस्य पालनीं
प्रजासु वृत्तिं यमयुङ्क्त वेदितुम् ।
स वर्णिलिङ्गी विदितः समाययौ
युधिष्ठिरं द्वैतवने वनेचरः ॥ १-१॥

The forest-dweller (the spy)
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Singular
Samasa वने चरति इति (उपपद-तत्पुरुषः / अलुक्-तत्पुरुषः)
Translation
"That forest-dweller (spy), bearing the guise of a Brahmacharin, whom (Yudhisthira) had appointed to find out the conduct—which maintains the prosperity of the lord of the Kurus (Duryodhana) towards his subjects—returned to Yudhisthira in the Dvaita forest, having known the facts."
