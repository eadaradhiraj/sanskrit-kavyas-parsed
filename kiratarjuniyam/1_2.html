कृतप्रणामस्य महीं महीभुजे
जितां सपत्नेन निवेदयिष्यतः ।
न विव्यथे तस्य मनो न हि प्रियं
प्रवक्तुमिच्छन्ति मृषा हितैषिणः ॥ १-२॥

Translation
"The mind of him (the spy), who had offered his salutations and who was about to report to the king (Yudhisthira) about the earth conquered by the enemy (Duryodhana), did not tremble. For well-wishers do not wish to speak words that are pleasing but false."
