अदाता पुरुषस्त्यागी दाता त्यागी च नित्यशः ।
इति ज्ञात्वा स्वयं बुद्ध्या धनं दद्यात्पुनः पुनः ॥ ११४४॥

Translation
"A non-giver (miser) ultimately becomes a renouncer (because he has to give up everything at the time of death), whereas a giver is regularly a renouncer (giving up bits of wealth constantly). Knowing this through one's own intelligence, one should give away wealth again and again."
