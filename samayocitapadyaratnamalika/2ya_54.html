अतीव बलहीनं हि लङ्घनं नैव कारयेत् ।
ये गुणा लङ्घने प्रोक्तास्ते गुणा लघुभोजने ॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
अतीव अतीव
Excessively / Extremely
बलहीनं बल-हीनं
Weak person (One destitute of strength)
Samasa बलेन हीनः (तृतीया-तत्पुरुषः)
हि हि
Indeed
लङ्घनं लङ्घनं
Fasting / Skipping meals
न न
Not
एव एव
At all (Emphatic)
कारयेत् कारयेत्
One should cause to do / Prescribe
Grammar Causal Optative (णिजन्ते विधिलिङ्)
ये ये
Which (Merits)
गुणाः गुणाः
Merits / Benefits
लङ्घने लङ्घने
In fasting
प्रोक्ताः प्र-उक्ताः
Are stated / Said
ते ते
Those (Same merits)
गुणाः गुणाः
Merits
लघुभोजने लघु-भोजने
In light eating / Light diet
Samasa लघु च तत् भोजनं, तस्मिन् (कर्मधारयः)
Translation
"One should not make an extremely weak person fast (undertake Langhana). The merits which are stated to exist in fasting, those very same merits are also found in light eating."
