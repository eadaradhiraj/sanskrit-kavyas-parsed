कायेन वाचा मनसापि शश्वद्
यत्संभृतं वासवधैर्यलोपि ।
आपाद्यते न व्ययमन्तरायैः
कच्चिन्महर्षेस्त्रिविधं तपस्तत् ॥ ५-५॥

That ascesis
Grammar Neuter, Nominative, Singular
Translation
"I hope that the threefold ascesis of that great sage—long accumulated by the exercise of his body, speech, and mind, and capable of disturbing even the firm courage of Indra—does not suffer waste by any kind of impediment!"
