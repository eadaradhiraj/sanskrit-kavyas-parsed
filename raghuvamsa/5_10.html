अपि प्रसन्नेन महर्षिणा त्वं सम्यग्विनीयानुमतो गृहाय ।
कालो ह्ययं सङ्क्रमितुं द्वितीयं सर्वोपकारक्षममाश्रमं ते ॥ ५-१०

The stage of life capable of benefiting all
Grammar क्षमम्: Adjective, Masculine, Accusative, Singular
आश्रमम्: Masculine, Accusative, Singular
Samasa सर्वेषाम् उपकारः इति सर्व-उपकारः (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः) । सर्व-उपकाराय क्षमम् इति सर्व-उपकार-क्षमम् (चतुर्थी-तत्पुरुषः)
ते तव
For you
Grammar Pronoun, Genitive, Singular (Base 'युष्मद्')
Translation
"I hope that the great, gracious sage (Varatantu), having thoroughly educated you, has permitted you to become a householder. For it is indeed time for you to enter into the second stage of life (Grihastha), which is capable of benefiting all."
