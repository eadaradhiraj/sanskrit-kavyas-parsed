मन्दोत्कण्ठाः कृतास्तेन गुणाधिकतया गुरौ ।
फलेन सहकारस्य पुष्पोद्गम इव प्रजाः ॥ ४-९॥

Translation
"By him (Raghu), due to the superiority of his virtues, the longing of the subjects for his father (Dilipa) was lessened; just as the longing for the mango blossoms is lessened by the (appearance of the superior) mango fruit."
