त्याजितैः फलमुत्खातैर्भग्नैश्च बहुधा नृपैः ।
तस्यासीदुल्बणो मार्गः पादपैरिव दन्तिनः॥ ४-३३॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
त्याजितैः त्याजितैः
By those made to give up / deprived
Grammar Masculine, Instrumental, Plural. Past Passive Participle of Causative (णिच् + क्त-प्रत्ययः) from root त्यज् (to abandon).
फलम् फलम्
Fruit / Wealth / Reward
Grammar Neuter, Accusative, Singular

Grammar Masculine, Instrumental, Plural. Past Passive Participle (क्त-प्रत्ययः) from root खन् with prefix उद्.
भग्नैः भग्नैः
By those broken / defeated
Grammar Masculine, Instrumental, Plural. Past Passive Participle (क्त-प्रत्ययः) from root भञ्ज्.

मार्गः मार्गः
The path / Route
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Singular
पादपैः पादपैः
By the trees
Grammar Masculine, Instrumental, Plural
इव इव
Like / As if
Grammar Indeclinable (अव्ययम्)
दन्तिनः दन्तिनः
Of an elephant
Grammar Masculine, Genitive, Singular (Base 'दन्तिन्')
Translation
"His formidable path was rendered conspicuous by kings who were deprived of their wealth, dethroned, and broken in various ways, just like the path of a wild elephant is marked by trees stripped of their fruit, uprooted, and broken."
