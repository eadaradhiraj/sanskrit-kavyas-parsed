ताम्रपर्णीसमेतस्य मुक्तासारं महोदधेः ।
ते निपत्य ददुस्तस्मै यशः स्वमिव सञ्चितम् ॥ ४-५०॥

Translation
"Having prostrated before him, they (the Pandya kings) presented him with the finest pearls extracted from the great ocean where it meets the river Tamraparni, offering it as if it were their own accumulated fame and glory."
