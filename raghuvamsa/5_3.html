Grammar Perfect Tense (लिट्), Parasmaipada, 3rd Person, Singular - Root वच्
Translation
"Then that Lord of men, Raghu, well-versed in duties and proper procedures, and standing foremost among those who regard honor as their only wealth, worshipped procedurally that young guest whose sole wealth was his ascesis. Standing nearby with folded palms towards the seated guest, the King spoke thus."
