तत्राक्षोभ्यं यशोराशिं निवेश्यावरुरोह सः ।
पौलस्त्यतुलितस्याद्रेरादधान इव ह्रियम्॥ ४-८०॥

Translation
"Having firmly established his unshakable mass of glory there on the Himalayas, he descended. By doing so, he appeared to bring deep embarrassment to Mount Kailash, the mountain that had once been easily shaken and lifted by Ravana (Paulastya)."
