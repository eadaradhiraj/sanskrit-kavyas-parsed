निदानमिक्ष्वाकुकुलस्य सन्ततेः सुदक्षिणा दौहृदलक्षणं दधौ ॥ ३-१॥

Translation
"Then Sudakshina bore the signs of pregnancy—signs that were deeply desired by her husband, whose fruit (the birth of a son) was imminent, which were like the delightful rise of moonlight to the expectant eyes of her female friends, and which were the fundamental cause for the continuation of the Ikshvaku lineage."
