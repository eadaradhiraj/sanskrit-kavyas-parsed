सार्धम् सार्धम्

Grammar Indeclinable (अव्ययम्) - Governs the Instrumental case.
आनताः आनताः
Bent down (in submission)
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Plural. Past Passive Participle (क्त-प्रत्ययः) from root नम् with prefix आ.
Translation
"The kings of Kamboja, unable to withstand his bravery in battle, bent down their heads in submission, along with their walnut trees which were bending and troubled by being used as tying halters for his war-elephants."
