कलत्रवन्तमात्मानमवरोधे महत्यपि ।
तया मेने मनस्विन्या लक्ष्म्या च वसुधाधिपः ॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
कलत्रवन्तम् कलत्र-वन्तम्
Possessing a wife
आत्मानम् आत्मानम्
Himself
अवरोधे अवरोधे
In the harem / inner apartments
महति महति
Large / Great
अपि अपि
Even though
तया तया
By her (Sudakshina)
मेने मेने
Considered / Thought
मनस्विन्या मनस्विन्या
By the high-minded / wise one
लक्ष्म्या लक्ष्म्या
By Lakshmi (Royal Fortune)
च च
And
वसुधाधिपः वसुधा-अधिपः
The Lord of the Earth (King)
Samasa वसुधायाः अधिपः (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः)
Translation
"Although he had a large harem, the Lord of the Earth considered himself to be truly married (possessed of a wife) only through her, the high-minded Sudakshina, and through Lakshmi (the Goddess of Fortune)."
