सेयं स्वदेहार्पणनिष्क्रयेण न्याय्या मया मोचयितुं भवत्तः ।
न पारणा स्याद्विहता तवैवं भवेदलुप्तश्च मुनेः क्रियार्थः ॥ २-५५॥

Translation
"Therefore, it is just for me to secure her release from you by paying the ransom of offering my own body. In this way, your meal (breaking of the fast) will not be obstructed, and the Sage's means for performing rituals will also remain intact."
