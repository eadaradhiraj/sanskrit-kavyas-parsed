आपादपद्मप्रणताः कलमा इव ते रघुम् ।
फलैः संवर्धयामासुरुत्खातप्रतिरोपिताः॥ ४-३७॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
आपादपद्मप्रणताः आ-पाद-पद्म-प्रणताः
Bowing down to his lotus feet / Bending down to the root
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Plural
Samasa पादौ एव पद्मौ (कर्मधारयः), आ पादपद्मम् (अव्ययीभावः), आपादपद्मं प्रणताः (द्वितीया-तत्पुरुषः)
कलमाः कलमाः
The rice plants
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Plural
इव इव
Like
Grammar Indeclinable (अव्ययम्)
ते ते
They (the Kings of Vanga)
Grammar Pronoun, Masculine, Nominative, Plural (Base 'तद्')
रघुम् रघुम्
Raghu
Grammar Masculine, Accusative, Singular
फलैः फलैः
With wealth / With grains
Grammar Neuter, Instrumental, Plural
संवर्धयामासुः संवर्धयामासुः
Made to prosper / Enriched
Grammar Periphrastic Perfect Tense (लिट्), Parasmaipada, 3rd Person, Plural - Root वृध् with prefix सम् and Causative (णिच्)
उत्खातप्रतिरोपिताः उत्खात-प्रतिरोपिताः
Uprooted and reinstated (replanted)
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Plural
Samasa उत्खाताश्च ते प्रतिरोपिताश्च (कर्मधारयः)
Translation
"Just like rice plants that yield abundant grain after being uprooted and replanted, those kings of Vanga, having been dethroned and then reinstated by Raghu, enriched him with immense wealth, bowing down with reverence to his lotus-like feet."
