शतैस्तमक्ष्णामनिमेषवृत्तिभिर्हरिं विदित्वा हरिभिश्च वाजिभिः ।
अवोचदेनं गगनस्पृशा रघुः स्वरेण धीरेण निवर्तयन्निव ॥ ३-४३॥

Translation
"Having recognized him to be Indra by his hundreds of unblinking eyes and his green horses, Raghu spoke to him with a deep, solemn voice that seemed to touch the sky, as if trying to stop him."
