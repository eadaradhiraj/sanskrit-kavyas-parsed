Translation
"At a certain time, Emperor Raghu performed a Vedic ritual called Vishvajit, in which he gave away all his accumulated wealth as donations, remaining almost empty-handed. Right at that time, Kautsa, a pupil of Sage Varatantu who had completed his education, approached the Emperor with an ambition to acquire wealth in order to pay the due fee to his preceptor."
