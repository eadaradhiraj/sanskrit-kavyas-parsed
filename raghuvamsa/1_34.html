सन्तानार्थाय विधये स्वभुजादवतारिता ।
तेन धूर्जगतो गुर्वी सचिवेषु निचिक्षिपे ॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
सन्तानार्थाय सन्तान-अर्थाय

Translation
"For the performance of the sacred rite to obtain progeny, he took down the heavy burden of the world from his own arm and placed it upon his ministers."
