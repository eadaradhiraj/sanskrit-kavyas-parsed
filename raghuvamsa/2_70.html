प्रातर्यथोक्तव्रतपारणान्ते प्रास्थानिकं स्वस्त्ययनं प्रयुज्य ।
तौ दंपती स्वां प्रति राजधानीं प्रस्थापयामास वशी वसिष्ठः ॥ २-७०॥

Translation
"In the morning, after the conclusion (breaking of the fast) of the vow performed exactly as prescribed, the self-controlled Sage Vasistha performed the auspicious rites for a safe journey and sent the couple (Dilipa and Sudakshina) off towards their own capital."
