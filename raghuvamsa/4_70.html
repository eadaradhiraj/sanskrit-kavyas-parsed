तेषां सदश्वभूयिष्ठास्तुङ्गा द्रविणराशयः ।
उपदा विविशुः शश्वन्नोत्सेकाः कोसलेश्वरम् ॥ ४-७०॥

Translation
"Lofty heaps of wealth consisting predominantly of excellent thoroughbred horses repeatedly reached the Lord of Kosala (Raghu) as tributes from them (the Kambojas); but pride never entered him."
