ततः समानीय स मानितार्थी हस्तौ स्वहस्तार्जितवीरशब्दः ।
वंशस्य कर्तारमनन्तकीर्तिं सुदक्षिणायां तनयं ययाचे ॥ २-६४॥

Translation
"Then, he (Dilipa)—who earned the title of 'Hero' by the strength of his own hands and who honors all suppliants—folded his hands and asked for a son to be born in Sudakshina; a son who would perpetuate his lineage and possess endless fame."
