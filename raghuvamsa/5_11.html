तवार्हतो नाभिगमेन तृप्तं मनो नियोगक्रिययोत्सुकं मे ।
अप्याज्ञया शासितुरात्मना वा प्राप्तोऽसि संभावयितुं वनान्माम् ॥ ५-११
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
तवार्हतो तव अर्हतः
Of you, the worthy one
Grammar तव: Pronoun, Genitive, Singular (Base 'युष्मद्')
अर्हतः: Present Participle (Shatr), Masculine, Genitive, Singular (Base 'अर्हत्')
नाभिगमेन न अभिगमेन
Not (just) by the arrival
Grammar न: Indeclinable (अव्ययम्)
अभिगमेन: Masculine, Instrumental, Singular
तृप्तं तृप्तम्
Satisfied
Grammar Adjective, Neuter, Nominative, Singular (Past Participle 'क्त')
मनो मनः
Mind / Heart
Grammar Neuter, Nominative, Singular (Base 'मनस्')
नियोगक्रिययोत्सुकं नियोग-क्रियया उत्सुकम्
Enthusiastic to perform the assigned duty
Grammar नियोग-क्रियया: Feminine, Instrumental, Singular
उत्सुकम्: Adjective, Neuter, Nominative, Singular
Samasa नियोगस्य क्रिया इति नियोग-क्रिया (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः), तया
मे मम
My
Grammar Pronoun, Genitive, Singular (Base 'अस्मद्')
अप्याज्ञया अपि आज्ञया
Whether by the order
Grammar अपि: Indeclinable (अव्ययम्)
आज्ञया: Feminine, Instrumental, Singular
शासितुरात्मना शासितुः आत्मना
Of the preceptor (mentor) or on your own accord
Grammar शासितुः: Masculine, Genitive, Singular (Base 'शासितृ')
आत्मना: Masculine, Instrumental, Singular (Base 'आत्मन्')
वा वा
Or
Grammar Indeclinable (अव्ययम्)
प्राप्तोऽसि प्राप्तः असि
You have arrived
Grammar प्राप्तः: Past Participle (Kta), Masculine, Nominative, Singular
असि: Root 'अस्' (as), Parasmaipada, Lat Lakara (Present Tense), Madhyama Purusha (2nd Person), Ekavacana (Singular)
संभावयितुं सम्भावयितुम्
To honor / grace
Grammar Root 'सम्-भू' (sam-bhū), Causative, Tumun Pratyaya (Infinitive)
वनान्माम् वनात् माम्
From the forest to me
Grammar वनात्: Neuter, Ablative, Singular (Base 'वन')
माम्: Pronoun, Accusative, Singular (Base 'अस्मद्')
Translation
"My mind is not fully content merely by the arrival of a worthy one like you, for it is highly enthusiastic to perform whatever duty you assign. Whether you have come from the forest to grace me by your preceptor's order or of your own accord, (command me)."
