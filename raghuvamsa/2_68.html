तस्याः प्रसन्नेन्दुमुखः प्रसादं गुरुर्नृपाणां गुरवे निवेद्य ।
प्रहर्षचिह्नानुमितं प्रियायै शशंस वाचा पुनरुक्तमेव ॥ २-६८॥

Translation
"The Master of Kings (Dilipa), whose face was as bright as the clear moon, reported her (Nandini's) favor to his Guru. Then, he told the news to his beloved wife; but since she had already inferred it from the visible signs of his extreme joy, his words served merely as a repetition."
