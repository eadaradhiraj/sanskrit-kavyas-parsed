शरीरमात्रेण नरेन्द्र तिष्ठन्नाभासि तीर्थप्रतिपादितर्द्धिः ।
आरण्यकोपात्तफलप्रसूतिः स्तम्बेन नीवार इवावशिष्टः ॥ ५-१५
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
शरीरमात्रेण शरीर-मात्रेण
With only the body
Grammar Neuter, Instrumental, Singular
Samasa शरीरं मात्रं यस्य सः शरीर-मात्रः, तेन (मयूरव्यंसकादि / बहुव्रीहिः)
नरेन्द्र नर-इन्द्र
O King of men
Grammar Masculine, Vocative, Singular
Samasa नराणाम् इन्द्रः इति नर-इन्द्रः (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः)
तिष्ठन्नाभासि तिष्ठन् आभासि
Standing, you appear
Grammar तिष्ठन्: Shatr-Pratyaya (Present Participle), Nominative Singular
आभासि: Root 'भा' with prefix 'आ', Lat Lakara, 2nd Person Singular
तीर्थप्रतिपादितर्द्धिः तीर्थ-प्रतिपादित-ऋद्धिः
One whose wealth was given to worthy recipients
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Singular
Samasa तीर्थेभ्यः प्रतिपादिता ऋद्धिः येन सः (बहुव्रीहिः)
आरण्यकोपात्तफलप्रसूतिः आरण्यक-उपात्त-फल-प्रसूतिः
Whose yield of grain was taken by foresters
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Singular
Samasa आरण्यकैः उपात्ता फलप्रसूतिः यस्य सः (बहुव्रीहिः)
स्तम्बेन स्तम्बेन
By the stalk / stem
Grammar Masculine, Instrumental, Singular
नीवार नीवारः
Wild rice plant (Nivara)
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Singular
इवावशिष्टः इव अवशिष्टः
Like what is remaining
Grammar अवशिष्टः: Masculine, Nominative, Singular (Kta-Pratyaya)
Translation
"O King! Having given away your entire wealth to worthy recipients and remaining now with only your body, you appear like a wild rice (Nivara) plant of which only the stalk remains after its yield of grain has been gathered by foresters."
