नृपस्य नातिप्रमनाः सदोगृहं सुदक्षिणासूनुरपि न्यवर्तत ॥ ३-६७॥

Translation
"Saying 'So be it', Indra (whose charioteer is Matali), having agreed to Raghu's request, returned the way he came. The son of Sudakshina (Raghu) also returned to the King's assembly hall, though not very delighted (as he failed to bring the horse)."
