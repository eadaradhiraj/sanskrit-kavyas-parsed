अभ्यभूयत वाहानां चरतां गात्रशिञ्जितैः ।
वर्मभिः पवनोद्धूतराजतालीवनध्वनिः ॥ ४-५६॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
अभ्यभूयत अभ्यभूयत
Was overcome / overpowered
Grammar Imperfect Tense (लङ्), Passive Voice (कर्मणि प्रयोग), Atmanepada, 3rd Person, Singular - Root भू with prefix अभि
वाहानाम् वाहानाम्
Of the horses
Grammar Masculine, Genitive, Plural
चरताम् चरताम्
Of the moving / marching
Grammar Masculine, Genitive, Plural (Adjective to वाहानाम्). Present Active Participle (शतृ-प्रत्ययः) from root चर् (to move).
गात्रशिञ्जितैः गात्र-शिञ्जितैः
By the jingling sounds on their bodies
Grammar Neuter, Instrumental, Plural
Samasa गात्रेषु शिञ्जितानि (सप्तमी-तत्पुरुषः), तैः
वर्मभिः वर्मभिः
By the armors
Grammar Neuter, Instrumental, Plural (Base 'वर्मन्')
पवनोद्धूतराजतालीवनध्वनिः पवन-उद्धूत-राजताली-वन-ध्वनिः
The sound of the groves of Rajatali palms rustled by the wind
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Singular
Samasa पवनेन उद्धूतानि (तृतीया-तत्पुरुषः), राजतालीनां वनानि (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः), पवनोद्धूतानि च तानि राजतालीवनानि च (कर्मधारयः), तेषां ध्वनिः (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः)
Translation
"The sound of the groves of Rajatali palms, rustled by the wind, was completely overpowered by the jingling of the armors on the bodies of his marching horses."
