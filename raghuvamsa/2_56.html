स्थातुं नियोक्तुर्न हि शक्यमग्रे विनाश्य रक्ष्यं स्वयमक्षतेन ॥ २-५६॥

Translation
"Being a dependent (servant) yourself, you surely understand this fact. Great indeed is your effort regarding this Devadaru tree. It is certainly not possible to stand before one's master (without shame) having failed to protect the charge, while remaining unhurt oneself."
