रघुवंशम्

Canto 4, Shloka 16

वार्षिकं सञ्जहारेन्द्रो धनुर्जैत्रं रघुर्दधौ ।
प्रजार्थसाधने तौ हि पर्यायोद्यतकार्मुकौ ॥

Translation

Indra withdrew his bow belonging to the rainy season (the rainbow), and Raghu took up his victorious bow. Indeed, for the purpose of accomplishing the welfare of the subjects, those two took up their bows alternately.
