दिलीपानन्तरं राज्ये तं निशम्य प्रतिष्ठितम् ।
पूर्वं प्रधूमितो राज्ञां हृदयेऽग्निरिवोत्थितः ॥ ४-२॥

Translation
"Having heard that he (Raghu) was established in the kingdom after Dilipa, the fire (of jealousy) in the hearts of the enemy kings, which was previously only smoldering, suddenly flared up."
