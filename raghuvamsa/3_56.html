जहार चान्येन मयूरपत्रिणा शरेण शक्रस्य महाशनिध्वजम् ।
चुकोप तस्मै स भृशं सुरश्रियः प्रसह्य केशव्यपरोपणादिव ॥ ३-५६॥

Translation
"And with another arrow feathered with peacock plumes, Raghu cut off Indra's banner which bore the emblem of the Thunderbolt. Indra became excessively angry at him, as if Raghu had forcibly pulled the hair of the Goddess of Divine Sovereignty (Sura-shri) herself."
