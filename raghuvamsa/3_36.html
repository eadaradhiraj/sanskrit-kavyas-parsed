अगच्छदंशेन गुणाभिलाषिणी नवावतारं कमलादिवोत्पलम् ॥ ३-३६॥

Translation
"Royal Glory (Lakshmi), who is fond of virtues, moved with a portion of herself from the King's main palace to the abode named 'Yuvaraja' (Raghu's residence); just as Lakshmi moves from the main Lotus to a freshly bloomed Lily."
