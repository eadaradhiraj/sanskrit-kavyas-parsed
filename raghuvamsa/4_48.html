Grammar Indeclinable (अव्ययम्)
Translation
"The neck-ropes of his elephants, which were fastened around the sandalwood trees specifically in the grooves formed by the coiling of snakes, did not slip down, even though those elephants were so strong they could easily snap their leg-fetters."
