नयविद्भिर्नवे राज्ञि सदसच्चोपदर्शितम् ।
पूर्व एवाभवत्पक्षस्तस्मिन्नाभवदुत्तरः ॥ ४-१०॥

Translation
"Though the experts in policy pointed out to the new King both the good (Sat) and the bad (Asat) courses of action (as theoretically possible), in him there existed only the former side (Goodness); the latter (Badness) was never found."
