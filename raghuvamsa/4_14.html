लब्धप्रशमनस्वस्थमथैनं समुपस्थिता ।
पार्थिवश्रीर्द्वितीयेव शरत्पङ्कजलक्षणा ॥ ४-१४॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
लब्धप्रशमनस्वस्थम् लब्ध-प्रशमन-स्वस्थम्
Who was at ease having obtained tranquility/pacification (of enemies)

The Autumn season
पङ्कजलक्षणा पङ्कज-लक्षणा
Distinguished by lotuses (holding a lotus like Lakshmi)
Samasa पङ्कजानि एव लक्षणानि (चिह्नानि) यस्याः सा (बहुव्रीहिः)
Translation
"Then, Autumn (Sharad)—distinguished by her lotuses—approached him, who was at ease having established peace (by subduing enemies), waiting upon him like a second Royal Fortune (Rajalakshmi)."
