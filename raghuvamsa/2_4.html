व्रताय तेनानुचरेण धेनोर्न्यषेधि शेषोऽप्यनुयायिवर्गः ।
न चान्यतस्तस्य शरीररक्षा स्ववीर्यगुप्ता हि मनोः प्रसूतिः ॥ २-४॥

मनोः प्रसूतिः मनोः प्रसूतिः
Offspring of Manu (Kings)
Translation
"For the sake of the vow, he, taking the role of the cow's attendant, sent back the rest of his retinue as well. Nor did he need protection of his body from others, for the progeny of Manu are protected by their own prowess."
