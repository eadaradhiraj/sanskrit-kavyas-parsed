प्रियानुरागस्य मनः समुन्नतेर्भुजार्जितानां च दिगन्तसंपदाम् ।
यथाक्रमं पुंसवनादिकाः क्रिया धृतेश्च धीरः सदृशीर्व्यधत्त सः ॥ ३-१०॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis

Of his love for his beloved
Samasa प्रियायाम् अनुरागः, तस्य (सप्तमी/षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः)

And
धीरः धीरः
The wise / steady one (Dilipa)
सदृशीः सदृशीः
Befitting / Worthy / Commensurate
व्यधत्त व्यधत्त
He performed / Arranged
सः सः
He
Translation
"That wise King performed the prenatal rites, beginning with Pumsavana, in due order—rites that were truly befitting the depth of his love for his beloved, the loftiness of his mind, the vast wealth of the empire earned by his own arms, and his overwhelming joy."
