अप्यग्रणीर्मन्त्रकृतामृषीणां
कुशाग्रबुद्धे कुशली गुरुस्ते ।
यतस्त्वया ज्ञानमशेषमाप्तं
लोकेन चैतन्यमिवोष्णरश्मेः ॥ ५-४॥

Translation
"Oh, keen-witted one! Is your preceptor, Sage Varatantu—the foremost among the seers of hymns—safe and hale? From him you have acquired complete knowledge, just as the world acquires the state of activity from the hot-rayed sun."
