तमभ्यनन्दत्प्रथमं प्रबोधितः प्रजेश्वरः शासनहारिणा हरेः ।
परामृशन्हर्षजडेन पाणिना तदीयमङ्गं कुलिशव्रणाङ्कितम् ॥ ३-६८॥

Translation
"The King (Dilipa), who had already been informed by Indra's messenger, welcomed Raghu, gently stroking his body—marked with the scars of the Thunderbolt—with a hand numbed by excessive joy."
