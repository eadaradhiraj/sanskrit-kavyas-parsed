Shloka 1.57
Prev Next
तयोर्जगृहतुः पादान्राजा राज्ञी च मागधी ।
तौ गुरुर्गुरुपत्नी च प्रीत्या प्रतिननन्दतुः ॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
तयोः तयोः
Of those two (Vasistha and Arundhati)
जगृहतुः जगृहतुः
Touched / Grasped (the feet)
पादान् पादान्
Feet
राजा राजा
The King
राज्ञी राज्ञी
The Queen
मागधी मागधी
Princess of Magadha (Sudakshina)
तौ तौ
Those two (King and Queen)
गुरुः गुरुः
The Guru (Vasistha)
गुरुपत्नी गुरु-पत्नी
The Guru's wife
प्रीत्या प्रीत्या
With affection
प्रतिननन्दतुः प्रतिननन्दतुः
Welcomed / Blessed
Translation
"The King and the Queen of Magadha touched the feet of the sage and his wife; and the Guru and the Guru's wife welcomed them with affection."
