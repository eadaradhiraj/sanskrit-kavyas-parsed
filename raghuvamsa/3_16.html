जनाय शुद्धान्तचराय शंसते
कुमारजन्मामृतसंमिताक्षरम् ।
अदेयमासीत्त्रयमेव भूपतेः
शशिप्रभं छत्रमुभे च चामरे ॥ ३-१६॥

छत्रम् छत्रम्
The Royal Umbrella
उभे उभे
The two
च च
And
चामरे चामरे
Fly-whisks (Royal Insignia)
Translation
"To the attendant of the harem who reported the birth of the prince with words sweet like nectar, everything belonging to the King was giveable (gifted away), except for three things: the moon-bright royal umbrella and the two fly-whisks."
