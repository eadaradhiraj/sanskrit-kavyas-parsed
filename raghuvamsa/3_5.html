Samasa उत्तरकोसलानाम् ईश्वरः (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः)
Translation
"The Princess of Magadha (Sudakshina) does not tell me anything she desires out of shyness. For what things is she longing?" — Thus, the attentive Lord of North Kosala (Dilipa) used to repeatedly ask her dear friends.
