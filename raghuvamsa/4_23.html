प्रसवैः सप्तपर्णानां मदगन्धिभिराहताः ।
असूययेव तन्नागाः सप्तधैव प्रसुस्रुवुः॥ ४-२३॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
प्रसवैः प्रसवैः
By the flowers / blossoms
Grammar Masculine, Instrumental, Plural

Of the Saptaparna trees
Grammar Masculine, Genitive, Plural
Samasa सप्त पर्णानि येषां ते (बहुव्रीहिः)

Grammar Masculine, Instrumental, Plural
Samasa मदस्य गन्धः इव गन्धः येषां तैः (बहुव्रीहिः)

His elephants

Grammar Indeclinable (अव्ययम्)
प्रसुस्रुवुः प्रसुस्रुवुः
Exuded / Flowed forth
Grammar Perfect Tense (लिट्), Parasmaipada, 3rd Person, Plural - Root स्रु (to flow) with prefix प्र
Translation
"Provoked by the flowers of the Saptaparna trees which smelled like elephant-rut, his elephants, as if out of jealousy, exuded rut in seven streams indeed."
