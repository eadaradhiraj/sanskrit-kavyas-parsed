सङ्ग्रामस्तुमुलस्तस्य पाश्चात्यैरश्वसाधनैः ।
शार्ङ्गकूजितविज्ञेयप्रतियोधे रजस्यभूत् ॥ ४-६२॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis

The battle
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Singular
तुमुलः तुमुलः
Fierce / Tumultuous
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Singular (Adjective to सङ्ग्रामः)
तस्य तस्य
His (Raghu's)
Grammar Pronoun, Masculine, Genitive, Singular

Who had horses (cavalry) as their military force
Grammar Masculine, Instrumental, Plural (Adjective to पाश्चात्यैः)
Samasa अश्वाः एव साधनं येषां ते (बहुव्रीहिः), तैः
शार्ङ्गकूजितविज्ञेयप्रतियोधे शार्ङ्ग-कूजित-विज्ञेय-प्रतियोधे
In which the enemy combatants could only be recognized by the twang of their bows
Grammar Neuter, Locative, Singular (Adjective to रजसि)
Samasa शार्ङ्गाणां कूजितम् (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः), तेन विज्ञेयाः (तृतीया-तत्पुरुषः), शार्ङ्गकूजितविज्ञेयाः प्रतियोधाः यस्मिन् तत् (बहुव्रीहिः), तस्मिन्
रजसि रजसि
In the (thick cloud of) dust
Grammar Neuter, Locative, Singular (Base 'रजस्')
अभूत् अभूत्
Happened / Took place
Grammar Aorist Tense (लुङ्), Parasmaipada, 3rd Person, Singular - Root भू (to be)
Translation
"A fierce battle took place between him (Raghu) and the Westerners (Persians), whose military strength lay in their cavalry, amidst a thick cloud of dust where the opposing warriors could only be recognized by the twang of their bows."
