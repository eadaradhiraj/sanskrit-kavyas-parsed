शशंस तुल्यसत्त्वानां सैन्यघोषेऽप्यसंभ्रमम् ।
गुहाशयानां सिंहानां परिवृत्यावलोकितम् ॥ ४-७२॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
शशंस शशंस
Indicated / Expressed
Grammar Perfect Tense (लिट्), Parasmaipada, 3rd Person, Singular - Root शंस् (to praise / express)
तुल्यसत्त्वानाम् तुल्य-सत्त्वानाम्
Of those possessing equal courage / strength
Grammar Masculine, Genitive, Plural (Adjective to सिंहानाम्)

In the noise of the army

Grammar Masculine, Genitive, Plural (Adjective to सिंहानाम्)
Samasa गुहायां शेरते इति (उपपद-तत्पुरुषः), तेषाम्
सिंहानाम् सिंहानाम्
Of the lions
Grammar Masculine, Genitive, Plural
परिवृत्य परिवृत्य
Having turned around
Grammar Indeclinable / Gerund (ल्यप्-प्रत्ययः) from root वृत् with prefix परि
अवलोकितम् अवलोकितम्
The backward glance / The looking
Grammar Neuter, Nominative, Singular. Past Passive Participle (क्त-प्रत्ययः) acting as a verbal noun.
Translation
"The backward glance, turning their heads around, of the lions resting in their caves indicated their complete lack of fear even amidst the deafening roar of the army, showing that they possessed courage equal to that of the king."
