निवर्त्य राजा दयितां दयालुस्तां सौरभेयीं सुरभिर्यशोभिः ।
पयोधरीभूतचतुःसमुद्रां जुगोप गोरूपधरामिवोर्वीम् ॥ २-३॥

Translation
"The compassionate King, fragrant with fame, having sent back his beloved wife, protected that daughter of Surabhi (Nandini), who resembled the Earth in the form of a cow with the four oceans as her udders."
