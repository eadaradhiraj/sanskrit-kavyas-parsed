पुरन्दरश्रीः पुरमुत्पताकं प्रविश्य पौरैरभिनन्द्यमानः ।
भुजे भुजङ्गेन्द्रसमानसारे भूयः स भूमेर्धुरमाससञ्ज ॥ २-७४॥

Translation
"He, possessing the splendor of Indra, having entered the city decorated with raised flags and being welcomed by the citizens, placed the burden of the earth (kingdom) once again upon his arm, whose strength was equal to that of the King of Serpents (Shesha)."
