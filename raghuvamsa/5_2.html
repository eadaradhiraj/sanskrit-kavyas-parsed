Translation
"Then that celebrated King Raghu, possessing an inestimable conduct and a naturally hospitable disposition, kept the respectful guest-offerings in an earthen vessel—because all his golden vessels had been donated in the Vedic ritual. Shining with glory, he welcomingly went forward to receive the young guest Kautsa, who was radiant with the enlightenment of the Vedas."
