कण्डूयमानेन कटं कदाचिद्वन्यद्विपेनोन्मथिता त्वगस्य ।
अथैनमद्रेस्तनया शुशोच सेनान्यमालीढमिवासुरास्त्रैः ॥ २-३७॥

Samasa असुराणां अस्त्रैः (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः)
Translation
"Once, the bark of this tree was stripped off by a wild elephant scratching its temple against it. Then, Parvati (the Daughter of the Mountain) grieved for it, just as she grieved for her son Skanda when he was wounded by the weapons of the demons."
