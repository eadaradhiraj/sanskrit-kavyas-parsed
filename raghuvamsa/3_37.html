विभावसुः सारथिनेव वायुना घनव्यपायेन गभस्तिमानिव ।
बभूव तेनातितरां सुदुःसहः कटप्रभेदेन करीव पार्थिवः ॥ ३-३७॥

Translation
"The King became exceedingly invincible (unbearable to enemies) because of him (Raghu); just as Fire becomes unbearable with the Wind as its charioteer, the Sun with the clearing of clouds, or an elephant with the flowing of rut-juice."
