दिशः प्रसेदुर्मरुतो ववुः सुखाः प्रदक्षिणार्चिर्हविरग्निराददे ।
बभूव सर्वं शुभशंसि तत्क्षणं भवो हि लोकाभ्युदयाय तादृशाम् ॥ ३-१४॥

Samasa लोकस्य अभ्युदयः, तस्मै (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः)
तादृशाम् तादृशाम्
Of such (great souls)
Translation
"The directions became clear; pleasant winds blew; the sacred fire, with its flames turning clockwise, accepted the oblation. At that moment, everything became indicative of auspiciousness; for the birth of such (great souls) is indeed for the prosperity of the world."
