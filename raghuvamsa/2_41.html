इति प्रगल्भं पुरुषाधिराजो मृगाधिराजस्य वचो निशम्य ।
प्रत्याहतास्त्रो गिरिशप्रभावादात्मन्यवज्ञां शिथिलीचकार ॥ २-४१॥

Grammar Chvi Pratyaya (अशिथिलां शिथिलां चकार)
Translation
"Having heard these bold words of the King of Beasts (the lion), the King of Men (Dilipa)—realizing that his weapon was repelled solely due to the power of Shiva (and not his own weakness)—loosened (let go of) the contempt he felt towards himself."
