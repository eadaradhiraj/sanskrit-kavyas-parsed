अन्येद्युरात्मानुचरस्य भावं जिज्ञासमाना मुनिहोमधेनुः ।
गङ्गाप्रपातान्तविरूढशष्पं गौरीगुरोर्गह्वरमाविवेश ॥ २-२६॥

Translation
"On the next day, wishing to test the devotion of her attendant, the Sage's cow entered a cave of the Himalayas (Gauri's father) where fresh grass was growing near the waterfall of the Ganges."
