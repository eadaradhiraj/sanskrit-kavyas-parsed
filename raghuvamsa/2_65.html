सन्तानकामाय तथेति कामं राज्ञे प्रतिश्रुत्य पयस्विनी सा ।
दुग्ध्वा पयः पत्रपुटे मदीयं पुत्रोपभुङ्क्ष्वेति तमदिदेश ॥ २-६५॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
सन्तानकामाय सन्तान-कामाय
To him who desired progeny
Samasa सन्तानस्य कामः यस्य सः, तस्मै (बहुव्रीहिः)

"So be it"

Grammar Absolutive (ल्यप्)

Translation
"Having promised the fulfillment of his wish by saying 'So be it' to the King who longed for a son, that milk-yielding cow directed him: 'O Son, milk my milk into a leaf-cup and drink it'."
