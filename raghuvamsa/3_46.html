तदङ्कमग्र्यं मघवन्महाक्रतोरमुं तुरङ्गं प्रतिमोक्तुमर्हसि ।
पथः श्रुतेर्दर्शयितार ईश्वरा मलीमसामददते न पद्धतिम् ॥ ३-४६॥

Translation
"Therefore, O Indra, you ought to return this horse, which is the principal limb of the great sacrifice. For the Great Lords, who show the path of the Scriptures to others, do not themselves adopt a sinful course of action."
