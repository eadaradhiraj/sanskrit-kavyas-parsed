ततः प्रजानां चिरमात्मना धृतां नितान्तगुर्वीं लघयिष्यता धुरम् ।
निसर्गसंस्कारविनीत इत्यसौ नृपेण चक्रे युवराजशब्दभाक् ॥ ३-३५॥

Translation
"Then, the King, wishing to lighten the extremely heavy burden of ruling the subjects which he had borne alone for a long time, appointed him (Raghu)—who was disciplined both by nature and by education—as the bearer of the title 'Crown Prince' (Yuvaraja)."
