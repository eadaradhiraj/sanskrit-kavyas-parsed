श्रियं महेन्द्रनाथस्य जहार न तु मेदिनीम्॥ ४-४३॥

Translation
"That righteous conqueror, King Raghu, took possession of the wealth of the Lord of Mahendra (the King of Kalinga)—who had been captured and then released—but he did not seize his territory."
