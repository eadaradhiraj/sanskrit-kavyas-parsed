दिशि मन्दायते तेजो दक्षिणस्यां रवेरपि ।
तस्यामेव रघोः पाण्ड्याः प्रतापं न विषेहिरे ॥ ४-४९॥

Translation
"Even the intense heat of the sun becomes mild in the southern direction (during its winter solstice); yet, in that very direction, the Pandya kings could not bear the fierce valor (the majestic heat) of King Raghu."
