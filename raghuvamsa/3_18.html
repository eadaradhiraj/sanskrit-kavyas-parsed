स जातकर्मण्यखिले तपस्विना
तपोवनादेत्य पुरोधसा कृते ।
दिलीपसूनुर्मणिराकरोद्भवः
प्रयुक्तसंस्कार इवाधिकं बभौ ॥ ३-१८॥

Translation
"When the entire birth-ceremony was performed by the ascetic family priest (Vasistha) who had arrived from the hermitage, Dilīpa's son shone even more brilliantly, like a gem from a mine that has been polished."
