वधूर्भक्तिमती चैनामर्चितामा तपोवनात् ।
प्रयता प्रातरन्वेतु सायं प्रत्युद्व्रजेदपि ॥ १-९०॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis

Wife (Sudakshina)

Also
Translation
"And let your wife, with devotion and purity, worship her and follow her in the morning as far as the woods, and go forth to welcome her in the evening."
