परस्परेण विज्ञातस्तेषूपायनपाणिषु ।
राज्ञा हिमवतः सारो राज्ञः सारो हिमाद्रिणा॥ ४-७९॥

Translation
"When those tribes approached holding tributes in their hands, a mutual recognition took place: the king became fully aware of the immense wealth possessed by the Himalayas, and the Himalaya mountain realized the formidable power of the king."
