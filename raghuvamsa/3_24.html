रथाङ्कनाम्नोरिव भावबन्धनं बभूव यत्प्रेम परस्पराश्रयम् ।
विभक्तमप्येकसुतेन तत्तयोः परस्परस्योपरि पर्यचीयत ॥ ३-२४॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis

Of the Chakravaka birds (Named after the chariot-wheel)

Translation
"The mutual love that existed between them (Dilipa and Sudakshina)—like the affection between a pair of Chakravaka birds—even though shared with their only son, actually increased towards each other (rather than diminishing)."
