अथ नयनसमुत्थं ज्योतिरत्रेरिव द्यौः
सुरसरिदिव तेजो वह्निनिष्ठ्यूतमैशम् ।
नरपतिकुलभूत्यै गर्भमाधत्त राज्ञी
गुरुभिरभिनिविष्टं लोकपालानुभावैः ॥ २-७५॥

Translation
"Then, for the prosperity of the King's lineage, the Queen conceived a child who was endowed with the heavy dignity of the Guardians of the Worlds (Lokapalas)—just as the Sky bore the light (Moon) born from Atri's eye, and as the Divine River (Ganga) bore the seed of Shiva cast by Agni."
