रघुवंशम्

Shloka 1.16
भीमकान्तैर्नृपगुणैः स बभूवोपजीविनाम् ।
अधृष्यश्चाभिगम्यश्च यादोरत्नैरिवार्णवः ॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis

By qualities both awe-inspiring and pleasing

Translation
"By reason of his kingly qualities which were both awe-inspiring and pleasing, he became at once unassailable and accessible to his dependents, even as the ocean is on account of its sea-monsters and gems."
