प्रजानां विनयाधानाद्रक्षणाद्भरणादपि ।
स पिता पितरस्तासां केवलं जन्महेतवः ॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis

केवलम् केवलम्

जन्महेतवः जन्म-हेतवः

Samasa जन्मनः हेतवः (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः)
Translation
"Because he disciplined them, protected them, and maintained them, he was the true father to his subjects; their biological fathers were merely the causes of their birth."
