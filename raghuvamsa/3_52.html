स एवमुक्त्वा मघवन्तमुन्मुखः करिष्यमाणः सशरं शरासनम् ।
अतिष्ठदालीढविशेषशोभिना वपुःप्रकर्षेण विडम्बितेश्वरः ॥ ३-५२॥

Samasa विडम्बितः ईश्वरः येन सः (बहुव्रीहिः)
Translation
"Having thus spoken to Indra, Raghu stood facing him, about to place an arrow on his bow. With his magnificent physique shining in the Alidha warrior posture, he appeared to imitate Lord Shiva himself."
