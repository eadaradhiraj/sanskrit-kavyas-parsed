रघुवंशम्

Shloka 1.19
सेना परिच्छदस्तस्य द्वयमेवार्थसाधनम् ।
शास्त्रेष्वकुण्ठिता बुद्धिर्मौर्वी धनुषि चातता ॥

Translation
"The army was merely a part of his retinue; his only two means of achieving his objects were: his intellect which was unobstructed in the Shastras, and the bow-string stretched on the bow."
