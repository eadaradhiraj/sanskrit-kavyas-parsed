ताम्बुलीनां दलैस्तत्र रचितापानभूमयः ।
नारिकेलासवं योधाः शात्रवं च पपुर्यशः॥ ४-४२॥

Translation
"There, having arranged improvised drinking spots, the warriors drank the coconut liquor using the leaves of the betel vine as cups; and in doing so, they seemed to quaff off the glory of their enemies as well."
