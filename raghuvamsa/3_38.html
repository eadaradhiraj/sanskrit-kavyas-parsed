नियुज्य तं होमतुरङ्गरक्षणे धनुर्धरं राजसुतैरनुद्रुतम् ।
अपूर्णमेकेन शतक्रतूपमः शतं क्रतूनामपविघ्नमाप सः ॥ ३-३८॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
नियुज्य नियुज्य
Having appointed

In the protection of the sacrificial horse
Samasa होमस्य तुरङ्गः (षष्ठी-त०), तस्य रक्षणं, तस्मिन् (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः)

शतं शतं
A hundred
क्रतूनां क्रतूनां

Translation
"Having appointed that archer (Raghu), followed by other princes, to protect the sacrificial horse, he (Dilipa)—who was equal to Indra—completed ninety-nine sacrifices (a hundred less by one) without any obstacle."
