त्रिलोकनाथेन सदा मखद्विषस्त्वया नियम्या ननु दिव्यचक्षुषा ।
स चेत्स्वयं कर्मसु धर्मचारिणां त्वमन्तरायो भवसि च्युतो विधिः ॥ ३-४५॥

Translation
"Indeed, by you—the Lord of the three worlds possessing divine vision—the enemies of sacrifice (demons) are always to be restrained. If you yourself become an obstacle in the religious rites of the righteous, then the established order is destroyed."
