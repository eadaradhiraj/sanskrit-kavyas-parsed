अल्पस्य हेतोर्बहु हातुमिच्छन्विचारमूढः प्रतिभासि मे त्वम् ॥ २-४७॥

Translation
"Sovereignty over the world under a single royal umbrella, young age, and this handsome body—wishing to sacrifice (all this) 'much' for the sake of 'little' (a cow), you appear to me to be foolish in your judgment."
