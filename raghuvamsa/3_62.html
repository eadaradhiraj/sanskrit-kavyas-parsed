तथापि शस्त्रव्यवहारनिष्ठुरे विपक्षभावे चिरमस्य तस्थुषः ।
तुतोष वीर्यातिशयेन वृत्रहा पदं हि सर्वत्र गुणैर्निधीयते ॥ ३-६२॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
तथापि तथा-अपि
Even so / Nevertheless
शस्त्रव्यवहारनिष्ठुरे शस्त्र-व्यवहार-निष्ठुरे
Which is cruel due to the usage of weapons

In the state of hostility / being an enemy

Samasa वीर्यस्य अतिशयः, तेन (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः)

Translation
"Nevertheless, Indra (the slayer of Vritra) was pleased with the extraordinary valor of Raghu, who had stood for a long time in the position of an enemy—a position cruel due to the use of weapons. For indeed, virtues establish a place for themselves everywhere (even in the heart of an enemy)."
