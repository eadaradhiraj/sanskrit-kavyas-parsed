The offspring / progeny (fawns)
Grammar Noun, Feminine, Nominative, Singular
Translation
"I hope the offspring of the female deer are safe and doing well—those fawns whose umbilical cords dropped off right in the bed-like laps of the sages, and whose desire to browse on the Kusha grass is not thwarted by the hermits out of sheer affection for them, even though that grass is an essential requisite for their religious rites."
