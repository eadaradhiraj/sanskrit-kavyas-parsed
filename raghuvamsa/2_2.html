मार्गं मनुष्येश्वरधर्मपत्नी श्रुतेरिवार्थं स्मृतिरन्वगच्छत् ॥ २-२॥

Translation
"The lawful wife of the Lord of men (Sudakshina), worthy of being cited first among chaste women, followed the path of that cow—a path made holy by the dust raised by her hoof-prints—just as the Smriti texts follow the meaning of the Shruti (Vedas)."
