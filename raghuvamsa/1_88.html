वन्यवृत्तिरिमां शश्वदात्मानुगमनेन गाम् ।
विद्यामभ्यसनेनेव प्रसादयितुमर्हसि ॥ १-८८॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
वन्यवृत्तिः वन्य-वृत्तिः

This

Translation
"Subsisting on forest fare, you should propitiate this cow by constantly following her personally, just as one masters Knowledge through constant application."
