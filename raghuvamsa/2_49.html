अथैकधेनोरपराधचण्डाद्गुरोः कृशानुप्रतिमाद्बिभेषि ।
शक्योऽस्य मन्युर्भवता विनेतुं गाः कोटिशः स्पर्शयता घटोध्नीः ॥ २-४९॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
अथ अथ
Moreover / If however

Due to (the loss of) a single cow

घटोध्नीः घट-ऊध्नीः
Having udders like water-pots (Rich in milk)
Samasa घटः इव ऊधः यासां ताः (बहुव्रीहिः)
Translation
"Moreover, if you are afraid of the Guru—who is fierce due to the offense of (losing) this single cow and who resembles fire—his anger can be pacified by you by presenting him with crores of other cows having udders as large as water-pots (yielding abundant milk)."
