भूर्जेषु मर्मरीभूताः कीचकध्वनिहेतवः ।
गङ्गाशीकरिणो मार्गे मरुतस्तं सिषेविरे ॥ ४-७३॥

Among the birch trees

Becoming rustling (making the 'marmara' sound)

Grammar Perfect Tense (लिट्), Atmanepada, 3rd Person, Plural - Root सेव् (to serve)
Translation
"On his mountain path, the gentle winds served him—winds that carried the cool water droplets of the river Ganga, that created a rustling sound among the leaves of the birch trees, and that acted as the breath for the musical sounds echoing through the hollow bamboos."
