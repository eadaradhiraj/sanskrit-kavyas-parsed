अनम्राणां समुद्धर्तुस्तस्मात्सिन्धुरयादिव ।
आत्मा संरक्षितः सुह्मैर्वृत्तिमाश्रित्य वैतसीम्॥ ४-३५॥

Of the unbowed / proud ones
Grammar Masculine, Genitive, Plural

Grammar Indeclinable / Gerund (ल्यप्-प्रत्ययः) from root श्रि with prefix आ
वैतसीम् वैतसीम्
Of the cane reed (yielding / bending)
Grammar Feminine, Accusative, Singular (Adjective to वृत्तिम्)
Translation
"The Suhmas protected themselves from him—the uprooter of the unbowed—just as one saves oneself from the fierce current of a river, by adopting the yielding nature of a cane reed (which bends rather than breaks)."
