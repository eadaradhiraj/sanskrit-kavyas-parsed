Translation
"Then, while the care of the pregnancy was being conducted by trustworthy physicians skilled in pediatrics (Kumara-bhritya), the delighted husband (Dilipa) saw his beloved wife facing delivery at the appropriate time, just as one sees the sky filled with clouds (ready to rain)."
