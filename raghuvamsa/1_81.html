सुतां तदीयां सुरभेः कृत्वा प्रतिनिधिं शुचिः ।
आराधय सपत्नीकः प्रीता कामदुघा हि सा ॥ १-८१॥

Translation
"Therefore, being pure and accompanied by your wife, worship her daughter (Nandini) as her representative; for she, when pleased, fulfills all desires."
