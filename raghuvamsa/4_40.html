प्रतिजग्राह कालिङ्गस्तमस्त्रैर्गजसाधनः ।
पक्षच्छेदोद्यतं शक्रं शिलावर्षीव पर्वतः॥ ४-४०॥

Translation
"The King of Kalinga, whose military strength consisted primarily of elephants, countered him (Raghu) with weapons, just as a mountain showering rocks would fiercely resist Lord Indra when he was ready to chop off its wings."
