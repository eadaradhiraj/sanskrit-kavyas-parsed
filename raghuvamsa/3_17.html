निवातपद्मस्तिमितेन चक्षुषा
नृपस्य कान्तं पिबतः सुताननम् ।
महोदधेः पूर इवेन्दुदर्शनाद्
गुरुः प्रहर्षः प्रबभूव नात्मनि ॥ ३-१७॥

Translation
"As the King drank in the lovely face of his son with a gaze steady like a lotus in still air, his immense joy could not be contained within himself, just as the swelling tide of the ocean cannot be contained upon seeing the moon."
