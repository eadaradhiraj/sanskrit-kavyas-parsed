प्रणिपातप्रतीकारः संरम्भो हि महात्मनाम् ॥ ४-६४॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
अपनीतशिरस्त्राणाः अपनीत-शिरस्त्राणाः
Having removed their helmets
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Plural

महात्मनाम् महा-आत्मनाम्
Of the noble-souled / great ones
Grammar Masculine, Genitive, Plural (Base 'महात्मन्')
Samasa महान् आत्मा येषां ते (बहुव्रीहिः), तेषाम्
Translation
"Taking off their helmets, the survivors sought refuge in him (Raghu); for indeed, the wrath of the high-souled is readily appeased by submission."
