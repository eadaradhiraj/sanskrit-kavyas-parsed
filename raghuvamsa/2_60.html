तस्मिन् क्षणे पालयितुः प्रजानामुत्पश्यतः सिंहनिपातमुग्रम् ।
अवाङ्मुखस्योपरि पुष्पवृष्टिः पपात विद्याधरहस्तमुक्ता ॥ २-६०॥

Translation
"At that moment, while the protector of the subjects (Dilipa) stood with his face bowed down, expecting the fierce attack of the lion, a shower of flowers released by the hands of Vidyadharas fell upon him."
