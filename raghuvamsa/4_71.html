ततो गौरीगुरुं शैलमारुरोहाश्वसाधनः ।
वर्धयन्निव तत्कूटानुद्धूतैर्धातुरेणुभिः ॥ ४-७१॥

Then / Thereafter

The father of Gauri (The Himalaya)
Grammar Masculine, Accusative, Singular (Adjective to शैलम्)

Grammar Masculine, Instrumental, Plural (Adjective to धातुरेणुभिः). Past Passive Participle (क्त-प्रत्ययः).
धातुरेणुभिः धातु-रेणुभिः
By the dust of minerals
Grammar Masculine, Instrumental, Plural
Samasa धातूनां रेणवः (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः), तैः
Translation
"Then, he (Raghu), whose army consisted largely of cavalry, ascended the mountain that is the father of Gauri (the Himalayas), as if increasing the height of its peaks with the mineral dust raised by the hooves of his horses."
