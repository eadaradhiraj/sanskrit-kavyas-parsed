स चापमुत्सृज्य विवृद्धमत्सरः प्रणाशनाय प्रबलस्य विद्विषः ।
महीध्रपक्षव्यपरोपणोचितं स्फुरत्प्रभामण्डलमस्त्रमाददे ॥ ३-६०॥

Translation
"Having cast aside his bow (which was useless now), he (Indra), with increased rage, took up the missile (Vajra) which was surrounded by a flashing halo of light and was capable of severing the wings of mountains, in order to destroy his powerful enemy."
