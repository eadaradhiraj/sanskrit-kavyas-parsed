स सैन्यपरिभोगेण गजदानसुगन्धिना ।
कावेरीं सरितां पत्युः शङ्कनीयामिवाकरोत्॥ ४-४५॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
स सः
He (Raghu)
Grammar Pronoun, Masculine, Nominative, Singular

By the army's enjoyment (bathing)

Translation
"By the plunging and bathing of his army, which made the waters fragrant with the rut-fluid (ichor) of his elephants, he rendered the river Kaveri as if she were an object of suspicion to her husband, the Lord of Rivers (the Ocean)."
