अथास्य गोदानविधेरनन्तरं विवाहदीक्षां निरवर्तयद्गुरुः ।
नरेन्द्रकन्यास्तमवाप्य सत्पतिं तमोनुदं दक्षसुता इवाबभुः ॥ ३-३३॥

Translation
"After the Godana ceremony (ritual hair-cutting), his father performed his marriage consecration. The princesses, having obtained him as a virtuous husband, shone resplendent like the daughters of Daksha (the constellations) having obtained the Moon (the dispeller of darkness)."
