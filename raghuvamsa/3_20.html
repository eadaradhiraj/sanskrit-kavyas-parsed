न संयतस्तस्य बभूव रक्षितुर्-
विसर्जयेद्यं सुतजन्महर्षितः ।
ऋणाभिधानात्स्वयमेव केवलं
तदा पितॄणां मुमुचे स बन्धनात् ॥ ३-२०॥

Grammar Perfect Tense (Lit Lakara) Atmanepada
Translation
"Under that Protector, there were no prisoners for him to release in celebration of his son's birth; thus, he himself was the only one released at that moment from the bond known as the 'Debt to the Ancestors'."
