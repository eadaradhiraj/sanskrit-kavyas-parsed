इति क्षितीशो नवतिं नवाधिकां महाक्रतूनां महनीयशासनः ।
समारुरुक्षुर्दिवमायुषः क्षये ततान सोपानपरम्परामिव ॥ ३-६९॥

Translation
"Thus, the Lord of the Earth, whose rule was praiseworthy, performed ninety-nine great sacrifices. Desiring to ascend to heaven at the end of his life, he seemed to have built a flight of stairs (with these sacrifices)."
