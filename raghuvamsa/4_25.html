तस्मै सम्यग्घुतो वह्निर्वाजिनीराजनाविधौ ।
प्रदक्षिणार्चिर्व्याजेन हस्तेनेव जयं ददौ ॥ ४-२५॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
तस्मै तस्मै
To him (Raghu)
Grammar Pronoun, Masculine, Dative, Singular (Base 'तद्')
सम्यग्घुतो सम्यक्-हुतः
Duly / well fed (with oblations)
Grammar सम्यक्: Indeclinable (अव्ययम्).
हुतः: Masculine, Nominative, Singular. Past Passive Participle (क्त-प्रत्ययः) from root हु (to offer / sacrifice).
Samasa सम्यक् हुतः (कर्मधारयः)
वह्निर् वह्निः
The ritual fire
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Singular (Base 'वह्नि')
वाजिनीराजनाविधौ वाजि-नीराजना-विधौ
In the lustration ceremony of the horses
Grammar Masculine, Locative, Singular
Samasa वाजिनां नीराजना (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः), वाजिनीराजनस्य विधिः, तस्मिन् (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः)
प्रदक्षिणार्चिर्व्याजेन प्रदक्षिण-अर्चिस्-व्याजेन
On the pretext of its flames swirling to the right
Grammar Masculine/Neuter, Instrumental, Singular
Samasa प्रदक्षिणा अर्चिः यस्य सः प्रदक्षिणार्चिः (बहुव्रीहिः - referring to the fire), तस्य व्याजः, तेन (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः)
हस्तेनेव हस्तेन-इव
As if with a hand
Grammar हस्तेन: Masculine, Instrumental, Singular (Base 'हस्त').
इव: Indeclinable (अव्ययम्).
जयं जयम्
Victory
Grammar Masculine, Accusative, Singular (Base 'जय')
ददौ ददौ
Gave / Conferred
Grammar Perfect Tense (लिट्), Parasmaipada, 3rd Person, Singular - Root दा (to give)
Translation
"In the lustration ceremony of his battle horses, the duly fed ritual fire gave him victory, as if with its own hand, on the pretext of its flames swirling to the right."
