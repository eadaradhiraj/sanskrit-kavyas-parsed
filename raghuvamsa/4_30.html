प्रतापोऽग्रे ततः शब्दः परागस्तदनन्तरम् ।
ययौ पश्चाद्रथादीति चतुःस्कन्धेव सा चमूः॥ ४-३०॥

Translation
"His valor went in front, then the sound, after that the dust, and lastly the chariots and other forces; thus, that army marched as if possessing four distinct divisions."
