भल्लापवर्जितैस्तेषां शिरोभिः श्मश्रुलैर्महीम् ।

With honeycombs
Grammar Neuter, Instrumental, Plural
Samasa क्षौद्रस्य पटलानि (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः), तैः

Translation
"He (Raghu) covered the ground with their severed, bearded heads—cut off by his spear-like arrows—which looked just like honeycombs swarming with bees."
