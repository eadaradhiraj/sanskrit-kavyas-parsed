ततो निषङ्गादसमग्रमुद्धृतं सुवर्णपुङ्खद्युतिरञ्जिताङ्गुलिम् ।
नरेन्द्रसूनुः प्रतिसंहरन्निषुं प्रियंवदः प्रत्यवदत्सुरेश्वरम् ॥ ३-६४॥

To the Lord of Gods (Indra)
Translation
"Then the Prince (Raghu), putting back the arrow which was only partially drawn from the quiver and which illuminated his fingers with the luster of its golden feathers, replied pleasantly to the Lord of Gods."
