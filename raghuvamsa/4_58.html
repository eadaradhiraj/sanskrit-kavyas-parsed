अपरान्तमहीपालव्याजेन रघवे करम् ॥ ४-५८॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis

To Parashurama

Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Singular. Past Passive Participle (क्त-प्रत्ययः) from root अर्थ् with prefix अभि.

Grammar Perfect Tense (लिट्), Parasmaipada, 3rd Person, Singular - Root दा (to give)

Under the pretext of the kings of the western coast (Aparanta)
Grammar Masculine, Instrumental, Singular
Samasa अपरान्तस्य महीपालाः (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः), तेषां व्याजः (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः), तेन
रघवे रघवे
To Raghu
Grammar Masculine, Dative, Singular
करम् करम्
Tax / Tribute
Grammar Masculine, Accusative, Singular
Translation
"Tradition has it that the ocean, when entreated, merely gave space (receded to give land) to Parashurama; but to Raghu, it offered tribute under the guise of the subjugated kings of the western coast (Aparanta)."
