स प्रतापं महेन्द्रस्य मूर्ध्नि तीक्ष्णं न्यवेशयत् ।
अङ्कुशं द्विरदस्येव यन्ता गम्भीरवेदिनः॥ ४-३९॥

Translation
"He (Raghu) firmly established his fierce valor on the peak of Mount Mahendra, just as a mahout plunges his sharp goad into the head of a stubborn elephant that is slow to feel the pain."
