अलं महीपाल तव श्रमेण प्रयुक्तमप्यस्त्रमितो वृथा स्यात् ।
न पादपोन्मूलनशक्ति रंहः शिलोच्चये मूर्च्छति मारुतस्य ॥ २-३४॥

Translation
"O Protector of the Earth! Enough of your effort. Even the weapon hurled by you would be useless against me. The force of the wind, which has the power to uproot trees, does not prevail against a mountain."
