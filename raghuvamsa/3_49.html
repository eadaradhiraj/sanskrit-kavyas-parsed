हरिर्यथैकः पुरुषोत्तमः स्मृतो महेश्वरस्त्र्यम्बक एव नापरः ।
तथा विदुर्मां मुनयः शतक्रतुं द्वितीयगामी न हि शब्द एष नः ॥ ३-४९॥

Translation
"Just as Hari alone is known as Purushottama, and Tryambaka (Shiva) alone is known as Maheshvara and no other; similarly, the sages know me alone as Shatakratu (The Performer of a Hundred Sacrifices). This title of mine does not go to a second person."
