रजोभिः स्यन्दनोद्धूतैर्गजैश्च घनसंनिभैः ।
भुवस्तलमिव व्योम कुर्वन्व्योमेव भूतलम्॥ ४-२९॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
रजोभिः रजोभिः
With the dust

Raised by the chariots
Grammar Neuter, Instrumental, Plural. 'उद्धूत' is Past Passive Participle (क्त-प्रत्ययः).
Samasa स्यन्दनैः उद्धूतानि (तृतीया-तत्पुरुषः), तैः

Grammar Masculine, Instrumental, Plural

Like

भूतलम् भू-तलम्
The surface of the earth
Grammar Neuter, Accusative, Singular
Samasa भुवः तलम् (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः)
Translation
"Making the sky look like the surface of the earth with the dust raised by the chariots, and rendering the earth's surface like the sky with elephants that resembled dense clouds, (he marched)."
