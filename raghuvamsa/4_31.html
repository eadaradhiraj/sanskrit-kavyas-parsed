मरुपृष्ठान्युदम्भांसि नाव्याः सुप्रतरा नदीः ।
विपिनानि प्रकाशानि शक्तिमत्त्वाच्चकार सः॥ ४-३१॥

The desert regions / surfaces

Grammar Pronoun, Masculine, Nominative, Singular
Translation
"Due to his immense power, he (Raghu) rendered the desert regions full of welling water, made the navigable rivers easy to cross, and cleared the dense forests making them bright and open."
