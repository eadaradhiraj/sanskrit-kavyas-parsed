स राज्यं गुरुणा दत्तं प्रतिपद्याधिकं बभौ ।
दिनान्ते निहितं तेजः सवित्रेव हुताशनः ॥ ४-१॥

Translation
"Having received the kingdom handed over by his father, he (Raghu) shone even more resplendently; just as Fire shines brighter at the end of the day when the Sun's lustre is deposited into it."
