Translation
"And he (Dilipa), who never transgressed the moral order, considered his lineage to be firmly established by this son of noble birth—just as the Lord of Creatures (Brahma) considers his own creation established by his own distinct form (Vishnu), who abides in the foremost of qualities (Sattva)."
