सत्रान्ते सचिवसखः पुरस्क्रियाभिर्-
गुर्वीभिः शमितपराजयव्यलीकान् ।
काकुत्स्थश्चिरविरहोत्सुकावरोधान्
राजन्यान्स्वपुरनिवृत्तयेऽनुमेने ॥ ४-८७॥

Grammar Perfect Tense (लिट्), Atmanepada, 3rd Person, Singular - Root मन् with prefix अनु
Translation
"At the conclusion of the sacrifice, Raghu (the descendant of Kakutstha), after consulting his ministers, appeased the humiliation of the defeated kings by conferring great honors upon them. He then permitted them to return to their own capitals, where their queens had grown eager and restless due to their prolonged separation."
