अथ वा मृदु वस्तु हिंसितुं मृदुनैवारभते प्रजान्तकः ।
हिमसेकविपत्तिरत्र मे नलिनी पूर्वनिदर्शनं मता ॥ ८-४५॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
अथ वा अथ वा
Or else / Alternatively
Grammar Indeclinables (अव्ययम्)
मृदु मृदु
Delicate / Soft
Grammar Neuter, Accusative, Singular (Adjective to 'वस्तु')

The lotus plant
Grammar Feminine, Nominative, Singular (Base 'नलिनी')
पूर्वनिदर्शनं पूर्व-निदर्शनम्
The first analogy / prior example
Grammar Neuter, Nominative, Singular
Samasa पूर्वं च तत् निदर्शनं च (कर्मधारयः)
मता मता
Is considered / thought
Grammar Feminine, Nominative, Singular. Past Passive Participle (क्त-प्रत्ययः) from root मन् (to think).
Translation
"Or else, the destroyer of living beings (Death) commences to destroy a delicate object with only a delicate one. Here, the lotus plant—whose destruction is brought about by a sprinkling of frost—is considered to be my first analogy."
