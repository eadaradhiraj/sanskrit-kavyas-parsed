रघुर्भृशं वक्षसि तेन ताडितः पपात भूमौ सह सैनिकाश्रुभिः ।
निमेषमात्रादवधूय च व्यथां सहोत्थितः सैनिकहर्षनिस्वनैः ॥ ३-६१॥

Translation
"Struck severely on the chest by that Thunderbolt, Raghu fell to the ground simultaneously with the tears of his soldiers. But, having shaken off the pain in a mere moment, he rose up again simultaneously with the joyous shouts of those very soldiers."
