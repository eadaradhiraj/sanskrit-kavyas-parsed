शरैरुत्सवसङ्केतान्स कृत्वा विरतोत्सवान् ।
जयोदाहरणं बाह्वोर्गापयामास किंनरान्॥ ४-७८॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
शरैः शरैः
With his arrows
Grammar Masculine, Instrumental, Plural
उत्सवसङ्केतान् उत्सव-सङ्केतान्
The Utsavasanketa tribes
Grammar Masculine, Accusative, Plural
स सः
He (Raghu)
Grammar Pronoun, Masculine, Nominative, Singular
कृत्वा कृत्वा
Having made
Grammar Indeclinable / Gerund (क्त्वा-प्रत्ययः) from root कृ
विरतोत्सवान् विरत-उत्सवान्
Devoid of festivities / Ceasing their festivals
Grammar Masculine, Accusative, Plural (Adjective to उत्सवसङ्केतान्)
Samasa विरताः उत्सवाः येषां ते (बहुव्रीहिः), तान्
जयोदाहरणं जय-उदाहारणम्
The song declaring his victory
Grammar Neuter, Accusative, Singular
Samasa जयस्य उदाहरणम् (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः)
बाह्वोर् बाह्वोः
Of his two arms
Grammar Masculine, Genitive, Dual (Base 'बाहु')
गापयामास गापयामास
Made (them) sing
Grammar Periphrastic Perfect Tense (लिट्), Parasmaipada, 3rd Person, Singular - Root गै with Causative (णिच्)
किंनरान् किंनरान्
The Kinnaras (celestial musicians)
Grammar Masculine, Accusative, Plural
Translation
"Having used his arrows to suppress the Utsavasanketa tribes, completely bringing their festivities to an end, he (Raghu) made the celestial Kinnaras sing the glories of the victories achieved by his arms."
