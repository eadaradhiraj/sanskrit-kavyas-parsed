उपेत्य सा दोहददुःखशीलतां यदेव वव्रे तदपश्यदाहृतम् ।
न हीष्टमस्य त्रिदिवेऽपि भूपतेरभूदनासाद्यमधिज्यधन्वनः ॥ ३-६॥

Translation
"Whenever she suffered from the discomfort of pregnancy-longings and asked for anything, she saw it instantly brought before her. For indeed, nothing desired was unattainable for this King—who always kept his bow strung—even in the heavens."
