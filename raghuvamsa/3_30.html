धियः समग्रैः स गुणैरुदारधीः क्रमाच्चतस्रश्चतुरर्णवोपमाः ।
ततार विद्याः पवनातिपातिभिर्दिशो हरिद्भिर्हरितामिवेश्वरः ॥ ३-३०॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
धियः धियः
Of the intellect
समग्रैः समग्रैः

स सः

ईश्वरः ईश्वरः

Translation
"Possessing a vast intellect and aided by all the qualities of mental retention, he mastered (crossed) the four branches of knowledge—which are comparable to the four oceans—in due order; just as the Lord of the Directions (the Sun) crosses the four quarters with his steeds that surpass the wind in speed."
