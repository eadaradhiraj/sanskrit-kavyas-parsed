आदानं हि विसर्गाय सतां वारिमुचामिव ॥ ४-८६॥

Translation
"He (Raghu) performed the Vishvajit sacrifice, in which one gives away their entire wealth as the ritual fee. For indeed, the wealth acquired by the magnanimous is solely meant for distribution, just like the water accumulated by the clouds is only meant to be showered down."
