उपपन्नं ननु शिवं सप्तस्वङ्गेषु यस्य मे ।
दैवीनां मानुषीणां च प्रतिकर्ता त्वमापदाम् ॥

Translation
"It is indeed but natural that there is welfare in the seven limbs of my state, since you are the averter of all calamities, both divine and human, that might befall me."
