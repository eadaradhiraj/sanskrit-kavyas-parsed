अरिष्टशय्यां परितो विसारिणा सुजन्मनस्तस्य निजेन तेजसा ।
निशीथदीपाः सहसा हतत्विषो बभूवुरालेख्यसमर्पिता इव ॥ ३-१५॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis

The bed in the lying-in chamber (delivery room)
Samasa अरिष्टस्य (सूतिकागृहस्य) शय्या, ताम् (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः)

आलेख्यसमर्पिताः आलेख्य-समर्पिताः
Placed in a painting (lifeless/static)
Samasa आलेख्ये समर्पिताः (सप्तमी-तत्पुरुषः)

Translation
"By the innate brilliance of that nobly born child, which spread all around the bed in the lying-in chamber, the lamps burning at midnight suddenly lost their lustre and appeared as if they were merely painted in a picture."
