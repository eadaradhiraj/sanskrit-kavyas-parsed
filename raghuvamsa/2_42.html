प्रत्यब्रवीच्चैनमिषुप्रयोगे तत्पूर्वभङ्गे वितथप्रयत्नः ।
जडीकृतस्त्र्यम्बकवीक्षणेन वज्रं मुमुक्षन्निव वज्रपाणिः ॥ २-४२॥

Grammar Desiderative Participle (Sannanta)

वज्रपाणिः वज्र-पाणिः
The Wielder of the Vajra (Indra)
Samasa वज्रं पाणौ यस्य सः (बहुव्रीहिः)
Translation
"And he (Dilipa), whose effort was rendered futile in the use of his arrow—a failure experienced by him for the very first time—replied to the lion; looking like Indra (Vajrapani) who, desiring to hurl his thunderbolt, was paralyzed by the mere glance of Lord Shiva."
