अतोऽयमश्वः कपिलानुकारिणा पितुस्त्वदीयस्य मयापहारितः ।
अलं प्रयत्नेन तवात्र मा निधाः पदं पदव्यां सगरस्य सन्ततेः ॥ ३-५०॥

Translation
"Therefore, imitating Kapila (who took away Sagara's horse), I have taken away this horse of your father. Enough of your effort in this matter! Do not set your foot on the path (of destruction) trodden by the sons of Sagara."
