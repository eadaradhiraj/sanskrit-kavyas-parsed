स त्वं मदीयेन शरीरवृत्तिं देहेन निर्वर्तयितुं प्रसीद ।
दिनावसानोत्सुकबालवत्सा विसृज्यतां धेनुरियं महर्षेः ॥ २-४५॥

महर्षेः महर्षेः
Of the Great Sage (Vasistha)
Translation
"Therefore, you—who are such (a servant of Shiva)—be pleased to satisfy the sustenance of your body (your hunger) with my body. Let this cow of the Great Sage be released, for her young calf is anxiously waiting for her at the end of the day."
