भेजे भिन्नकटैर्नागैरन्यानुपरुरोध यैः ॥ ४-८३॥

यैः यैः
With which
Grammar Pronoun, Masculine, Instrumental, Plural
Translation
"The lord of Kamarupa paid homage to Raghu—whose valor surpassed even that of Indra—by presenting him with those very infuriated elephants with exuding temples, by using which he had previously stalled other invaders."
