पारसीकांस्ततो जेतुं प्रतस्थे स्थलवर्त्मना ।
इन्द्रियाख्यानिव रिपूंस्तत्त्वज्ञानेन संयमी ॥ ४-६०॥

The Persians

Grammar Neuter, Instrumental, Singular
Samasa तत्त्वस्य ज्ञानम् (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः), तेन
संयमी संयमी
An ascetic / A self-controlled person
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Singular (Base 'संयमिन्')
Translation
"Then he (Raghu) set out by the land route to conquer the Persians, just as a self-controlled ascetic sets out to conquer the internal enemies known as the senses through the knowledge of the ultimate truth."
