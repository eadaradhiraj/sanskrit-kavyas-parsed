किमप्यहिंस्यस्तव चेन्मतोऽहं यशःशरीरे भव मे दयालुः ।
एकान्तविध्वंसिषु मद्विधानां पिण्डेष्वनास्था खलु भौतिकेषु ॥ २-५७॥

Translation
"If, moreover, you consider me not to be killed (out of respect for kingship), then show your compassion towards my 'body of fame'. For indeed, people like me have no regard for these material bodies composed of elements, which are destined to perish inevitably."
