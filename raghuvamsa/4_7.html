तथाप्यनन्यपूर्वेव तस्मिन्नासीद्वसुन्धरा ॥ ४-७॥

तस्मिन् तस्मिन्

The Earth
Translation
"Although the Earth had been ruled (enjoyed) by venerable kings beginning with Manu, yet, under Raghu's rule, she appeared as if she had never belonged to anyone before (like a fresh bride)."
