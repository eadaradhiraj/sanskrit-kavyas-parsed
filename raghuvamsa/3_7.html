क्रमेण निस्तीर्य च दोहदव्यथां प्रचीयमानावयवा रराज सा ।
पुराणपत्रापगमादनन्तरं लतेव संनद्धमनोज्ञपल्लवा ॥ ३-७॥

Translation
"Having gradually overcome the distress of pregnancy-longings, and with her limbs becoming fuller (well-nourished), she shone like a creeper which, immediately after shedding its old leaves, has put forth beautiful new sprouts."
