परिकल्पितसांनिध्या काले काले च बन्दिषु ।
स्तुत्यं स्तुतिभिरर्थ्याभिरुपतस्थे सरस्वती ॥ ४-६॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
परिकल्पितसांनिध्या परिकल्पित-सांनिध्या
Whose presence was fixed/arranged
Samasa परिकल्पितं सांनिध्यं यस्याः सा (बहुव्रीहिः)
काले काले काले काले
At every proper occasion
Grammar Vipsa (Weepsā - Repetition)
च च
And
बन्दिषु बन्दिषु
In the bards / panegyrists
स्तुत्यं स्तुत्यं
The praiseworthy one (Raghu)
स्तुतिभिः स्तुतिभिः
With praises / hymns
अर्थ्याभिः अर्थ्याभिः
Meaningful / Significant
उपतस्थे उपतस्थे
Waited upon / Served
Grammar Perfect Tense (लिट्) - Atmanepada
सरस्वती सरस्वती
Goddess of Speech
Translation
"Goddess Saraswati (Speech), having established her presence in the bards at every appropriate occasion, served the praiseworthy King (Raghu) with meaningful praises."
