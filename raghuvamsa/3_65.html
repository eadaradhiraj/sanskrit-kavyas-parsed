अमोच्यमश्वं यदि मन्यसे प्रभो ततः समाप्ते विधिनैव कर्मणि ।
अजस्रदीक्षाप्रयतः स मद्गुरुः क्रतोरशेषेण फलेन युज्यताम् ॥ ३-६५॥

Grammar Passive Imperative (कर्मणि लोट्)
Translation
"O Lord, if you consider the horse not to be returned, then may my father, who is purified by constant consecration, be endowed with the full fruit of the sacrifice, just as if the rite had been completed according to the rules."
