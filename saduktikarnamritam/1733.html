अदयं घर्ष शिलायां दह्यतां दाहेन भिन्धि लौहेन ।
हे हेमकार कनकं मा मां गुञ्जाफलैस्तोलय ॥ १७३३॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
अदयम् अ-दयम्
Mercilessly / Without pity
Grammar Adverb (Indeclinable) / Neuter, Accusative, Singular
Samasa न विद्यते दया यस्मिन् कर्मणि तत् यथा तथा (नञ्-बहुव्रीहिः / अव्ययीभावः)
घर्ष घर्ष
Rub / Grind
Grammar Imperative (लोट्), Parasmaipada, 2nd Person, Singular - Root घृष् (to rub)
शिलायाम् शिलायाम्
On the stone / On the touchstone
Grammar Feminine, Locative, Singular (Base 'शिला')
दह्यताम् दह्यताम्
Let (me) be burnt
Grammar Imperative (लोट्), Passive Voice (कर्मणि प्रयोग), Atmanepada, 3rd Person, Singular - Root दह् (to burn)
दाहेन दाहेन
With burning / With flames
Grammar Masculine, Instrumental, Singular (Base 'दाह')
भिन्धि भिन्धि
Break / Cut
Grammar Imperative (लोट्), Parasmaipada, 2nd Person, Singular - Root भिद् (to break / cut)
लौहेन लौहेन
With iron (tools)
Grammar Neuter, Instrumental, Singular (Base 'लौह')
हे हे
O!
Grammar Indeclinable (अव्ययम्)
हेमकार हेम-कार
O goldsmith!
Grammar Masculine, Vocative, Singular
Samasa हेम करोति इति (उपपद-तत्पुरुषः)
कनकम् कनकम्
Gold
Grammar Neuter, Accusative/Nominative, Singular
मा मा
Do not
Grammar Indeclinable (अव्ययम् - used for prohibition)
माम् माम्
Me
Grammar Pronoun, Accusative, Singular (Base 'अस्मद्')
गुञ्जाफलैः गुञ्जा-फलैः
With the rosary peas (Gunja seeds, used as tiny weights)
Grammar Neuter, Instrumental, Plural
Samasa गुञ्जायाः फलानि (षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः), तैः
तोलय तोलय
Weigh
Grammar Imperative (लोट्), Parasmaipada, 2nd Person, Singular - Root तुल् with Causative (णिच्)
Translation
"O goldsmith! Rub me, the gold, mercilessly on the touchstone, burn me with flames, or cut me with iron tools, but do not weigh me against the lowly rosary peas (Gunja seeds)!"
