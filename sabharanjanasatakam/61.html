अधीयते विजानन्ति विरज्यन्ति मुहुर्मुहुः ।
नात्यन्ताय निवर्तन्ते नरा वैषम्यतो विधेः ॥ ६१
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
अधीयते अधीयते
They study
Grammar Root 'इ' with prefix 'अधि' (to study), Atmanepada, Lat Lakara (Present Tense), Prathama Purusha (3rd Person), Bahuvacana (Plural)
विजानन्ति विजानन्ति
They understand / know
Grammar Root 'ज्ञा' with prefix 'वि', Parasmaipada, Lat Lakara (Present Tense), Prathama Purusha (3rd Person), Bahuvacana (Plural)
विरज्यन्ति विरज्यन्ति
They lose passion / get unattached
Grammar Root 'रञ्ज्' with prefix 'वि', Parasmaipada, Lat Lakara (Present Tense), Prathama Purusha (3rd Person), Bahuvacana (Plural)

निवर्तन्ते निवर्तन्ते
They turn away (from worldly life) / get liberated
Grammar Root 'वृत्' with prefix 'नि', Atmanepada, Lat Lakara (Present Tense), Prathama Purusha (3rd Person), Bahuvacana (Plural)
नरा नराः
People / Men
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Plural (Base 'नर')
वैषम्यतो वैषम्यतः
Due to the inequality / vagaries / adversity
Grammar Ablative Singular / Adverbial form with -tasi (तः) suffix, derived from abstract noun 'वैषम्य' (विषम + ष्यञ्)
विधेः विधेः
Of fate / destiny / creator
Grammar Masculine, Genitive, Singular (Base 'विधि')
Translation

English: People study and understand liberation and get unattached repeatedly, but they do not attain absolute and final liberation due to the vagaries and adversity of fate.

Hindi: लोग मोक्ष (निवृत्ति) का अध्ययन करते हैं, उसे समझते हैं, और बार-बार संसार से विरक्त भी होते हैं; परन्तु भाग्य के उतार-चढ़ाव (वैषम्य) के कारण वे पूर्ण रूप से निवृत्त (मुक्त) नहीं हो पाते हैं।
