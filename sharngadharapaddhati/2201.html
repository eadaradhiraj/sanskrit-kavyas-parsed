शार्ङ्गधरपद्धतिः

Shloka 2201 (Water Divining)
Prev Next
अतृणे सतृणा यस्मिन् सतृणे तृणवर्जिता मही यत्र ।
तस्मिञ्शिरा प्रदिष्टा वक्तव्यं वा धनं तत्र ॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
अतृणे अ-तृणे
In a place usually devoid of grass
Samasa न विद्यते तृणं यस्मिन् (बहुव्रीहिः)
सतृणा स-तृणा
Having grass / Grassy
Samasa तृणेन सह वर्तते (बहुव्रीहिः)
यस्मिन् यस्मिन्
In which (spot)
सतृणे स-तृणे
In a place usually full of grass
तृणवर्जिता तृण-वर्जिता
Devoid of grass
Samasa तृणैः वर्जिता (तृतीया-तत्पुरुषः)
मही मही
The ground / Earth
यत्र यत्र
Where
तस्मिन् तस्मिन्
In that (place)
शिरा शिरा
Vein (Underground water stream)
प्रदिष्टा प्रदिष्टा
Is indicated / Pointed out
वक्तव्यम् वक्तव्यम्
Should be declared / Said
वा वा
Or
धनम् धनम्
Treasure / Wealth
तत्र तत्र
There
Translation
"In a place where grass usually does not grow, if the ground is grassy; or where in a grassy area, the ground is devoid of grass—in that place, an underground stream of water is indicated, or it should be declared that a treasure lies there."
