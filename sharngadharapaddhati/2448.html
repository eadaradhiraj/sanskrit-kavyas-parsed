Translation
"A female jackal howling in the southern direction at night is an extreme giver of happiness and auspiciousness. However, that same jackal, appearing in the eastern direction in the morning, causes the city's governor to be replaced by another (an omen indicating political change or increased crime leading to a change in leadership)."
