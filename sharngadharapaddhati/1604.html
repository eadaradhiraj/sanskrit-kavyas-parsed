Shloka 1604

अथागत्य भुवं राज्ञां गता वाहनतां हयाः ।
तेषां धर्मार्थकामांश्च साधयन्त्युपकारिणः ॥ १६०४॥

Having come

भुवम् भुवम्

Accomplish / Fulfill
Grammar Present Tense (लट्), Parasmaipada, 3rd Person, Plural - Root साध् (to accomplish) in Causative (णिच्)
उपकारिणः उपकारिणः
The beneficial ones / Benefactors
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Plural (Base 'उपकारिन्')
Translation
"Thus, the horses, having come to the world (afterlife), have attained the state of being vehicles for kings. The beneficial horses accomplish their (the kings') dharma, prosperity, and desires."
