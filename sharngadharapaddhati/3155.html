हेलाविच्छित्तिविश्वोककिलिकिञ्चितविभ्रमाः ।
लीला विलासो हावश्च विक्षेपो विहतं मदः ॥ ३१५५॥
Padaccheda & Samasa Analysis
हेलाविच्छित्तिविश्वोककिलिकिञ्चितविभ्रमाः हेला-विच्छित्ति-विश्वोक-किलिकिञ्चित-विभ्रमाः
Passion, careless adornment, indifference, mixed emotions, and flurry
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Plural
Samasa हेला च विच्छित्तिश्च विश्वोकश्च किलिकिञ्चितं च विभ्रमश्च (इतरेतर-द्वन्द्वः), ते

Playful imitation (of the lover)
Grammar Feminine, Nominative, Singular
विलासः विलासः
Amorous grace / Graceful movement
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Singular
हावः हावः
Flirtatious expression
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Singular
च च
And
Grammar Indeclinable (अव्ययम्)
विक्षेपः विक्षेपः
Casting of glances / Distraction
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Singular
विहतम् विहतम्
Concealment of feelings / Bashfulness (Vihrita)

Intoxication / Pride of youth
Grammar Masculine, Nominative, Singular
Translation
"Hela (passionate expression), Vicchitti (carelessness in dress enhancing beauty), Vishvoka (indifference due to pride), Kilikinchita (a mixture of weeping, laughing, anger, etc.), Vibhrama (flurry or confusion in speech and dress), Lila (playful imitation of the lover), Vilasa (amorous grace), Hava (flirtatious gesture), Vikshepa (distracted casting of glances), Vihata (concealment of emotions), and Mada (intoxication/pride of youth)...
