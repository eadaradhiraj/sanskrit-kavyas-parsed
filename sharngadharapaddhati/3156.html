Translation
"...Moddayita (the awakening of love at the mention of the beloved), Kuddamita (feigned distress when hair or breasts are touched), Maugdhya (assumed innocence), Tapana (pining/burning for the lover), and Lalita (graceful swaying of the limbs)—thus, these are the amorous gestures and actions born from the emotion of love (Shringara)."
